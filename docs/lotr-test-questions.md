# Lord of the Rings Intelligence System Test Questions

These questions are designed to test an intelligence system's ability to provide relevant context to an LLM. They target details that would be difficult to answer correctly without source material, even for an LLM trained on Tolkien's works.

## Specific Details (Requires Exact Knowledge)

1. What was the name of Bilbo's mother, and why was her lineage significant?
2. How many years did Gollum possess the Ring before Bilbo found it?
3. What were the names of the two lamps that lit Middle-earth before the Trees?
4. What specific gift did Galadriel give to Gimli, and why was this significant?
5. What were the Seven Names of Gondolin?

## Minor Characters (Easily Forgotten)

6. Who was Beregond, and what law did he break at Minas Tirith?
7. What happened to Fatty Bolger after Frodo left the Shire?
8. Who was Ghân-buri-Ghân, and how did he aid Rohan?
9. What was the name of the Ent who guarded Isengard with Treebeard after its fall?
10. Who was Erestor, and what did he suggest at the Council of Elrond?

## Genealogy/Relationships (Requires Tracing)

11. How was Elrond related to both Aragorn and Arwen?
12. What choice did Elrond's brother make, and what was his name?
13. Through which ancestor did Aragorn descend from Lúthien?
14. Who were Fëanor's seven sons, and which survived the longest?

## Chronology (Requires Precision)

15. How many years passed between the forging of the One Ring and its destruction?
16. In what year of the Third Age did the Battle of Five Armies occur?
17. How long was the siege of Barad-dûr at the end of the Second Age?
18. What was the gap in years between Bilbo's "eleventy-first" birthday and Frodo's departure?

## Misleading/Nuanced (Where Training May Produce Wrong Answers)

19. How many Nazgûl were at the Ford of Bruinen? (Not nine)
20. Did Legolas ever miss a shot with his bow in the text of LotR?
21. What color was Gandalf's cloak when he was Gandalf the Grey? (Not grey)
22. How many of the Seven Rings given to Dwarves were recovered by Sauron?
23. Did Gimli actually receive three hairs from Galadriel, or was that just his request?

## Deep Lore (Silmarillion-Level)

24. What was the name of Morgoth's lieutenant who became the Dark Lord of the Second and Third Ages?
25. Who was Eärendil, and what celestial body did he become?
26. What were the names of the Two Trees of Valinor?
27. Who slew Glaurung, and what was his relationship to that dragon?
28. What was the Oath of Fëanor, and why did it doom his house?

## Reasoning/Motivation (Requires Understanding, Not Just Recall)

29. Why did the Ring have no power over Tom Bombadil?
30. Why couldn't Gandalf or Galadriel wield the Ring against Sauron?
31. What was the original purpose of the palantíri, and who made them?
32. Why did Sauron take the form of a fair being when he deceived the Elves?
33. Why were the Ents fading, and what did they lose?

## Linguistic (Requires Specific Knowledge)

34. What does "Moria" mean in Sindarin, and who gave it that name?
35. What is the Sindarin name for Rivendell, and what does it mean?
36. What language is "Baruk Khazâd!" and what does the full war-cry mean?
37. What was Gandalf's name in Valinor before he came to Middle-earth?
