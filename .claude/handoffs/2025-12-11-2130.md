## Insights & Patterns

- tRPC v10 adapter is at `@trpc/server/adapters/express` (no separate package)
- Vue/Vite path aliases need BOTH vite.config.ts AND tsconfig.json configuration
- Root-level gitignore patterns like `lib/` can accidentally exclude `src/lib/` - use `/lib/`
