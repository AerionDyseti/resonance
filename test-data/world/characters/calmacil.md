# Calmacil

**Calmacil** was the eighteenth King of Gondor and the brother of Narmacil I, whom he succeeded as King in TA 1294. 

## Biography

His elder son was Minalcar (aka Rómendacil II) and his younger son was Calimehtar. His reign lasted for ten years, during which his son Minalcar continued to rule as Regent, an office he had held during Narmacil's reign.

In TA 1304 Calmacil died and was succeeded by Minalcar.
