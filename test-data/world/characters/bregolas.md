# Bregolas

**Bregolas** was one of the Edain of the House of Bëor. He was the third Lord of Ladros.

## Biography
Bregolas was the brother of Barahir and Bregil, and the son of Bregor. He became the third Lord of Ladros after the death of his father. He was killed in the Dagor Bragollach defending Dorthonion with Angrod and Aegnor.
