**Sabrina Medujet** is a **Human Barbarian** and one of the player characters in the **"Keys to Perfection"** campaign. As an **Akaru nomad**, she represents the wild, uncivilized perspective within the highly ordered society of **Lux**.

## Background

**Akaru Nomad Heritage:**

- Member of the **Akaru** - a nomadic human culture
- Raised outside the structured civilizations of **Solaarus** and **Highwind**
- Embodies the **wild, free** lifestyle of the nomadic peoples
- Brings a **barbarian's perspective** to civilized society

**Outsider Status:**

- **Complete outsider** to Solaaran culture and customs
- Unfamiliar with the **Nine Caste system** and urban sophistication
- May struggle with or reject **civilized conventions**
- Represents the **untamed** aspects of humanity

**Cultural Clash:**

- **Individual freedom** vs. **social structure**

## Campaign History (Sessions 1–10)

### The Case of the Missing Falcon (Sessions 1–2)

Sabrina's identity was revealed during negotiations with Lady Sophis Medjay: she is **Saffiya Seshan**, which complicated the diplomatic situation around the missing gyr falcon Iri. The party learned of organized surveillance in Lux during this period.

### The Tainted Water Investigation (Sessions 3–10)

Sabrina's desert experience proved valuable as the party tracked Vek-Khar through the Solaar Desert. Her familiarity with harsh environments complemented Shemsu's guidance.

In the desert cavern (Session 5), the Dark Slayer attacked Sabrina with spectral tendrils, feeding on her life essence. Kales charged to her defense in a pivotal moment.

During the oasis dungeon exploration:

- Session 7: Sabrina triggered combat with Echo Wights by interacting with temple wall drawings; raged and pressed aggressive attacks despite enemy resistances
- Session 10: When Zeribella was possessed by the cursed spear, Sabrina attempted to grapple her to prevent harm; the party successfully contained the threat

The Dark Slayer bore tribal tattoos from a Hinandyr barbarian clan—nomadic warriors comparable to Sabrina's own Akaru people. This cultural connection between Hyrian and Akaru warrior traditions was noted.

### Items Acquired

- Dragon Scale Amulet of Natural Armor +1 (Session 10)

### Session 11: Sisters and Protectors

**The Revelation**: During the campfire scene, Sabrina revealed a crucial piece of information: **she and Zuleia are sisters**. This was the first time the party learned of their family connection.

**Power Awakening**: Sabrina shared a formative memory—at age eight, in a fit of anger, she broke a table when unexpected power manifested within her. "The power they wanted me to have was not the power I got," she explained. Her family's disappointment made her feel like "a problem child," and she "caved under the pressure." She continues to feel she caves under pressure.

**Comfort for Zeribella**: When Zeribella struggled to process the spear's traumatic memories, Sabrina offered surprisingly gentle comfort: "Those feelings are feelings that come from desperation. They're not the soul of a person. It's just the shadows that hide in the soul. It's okay." She later left chocolate and an extra blanket for Zeribella—"the Zuli treatment."

**The Protector's Declaration**: Sabrina declared she would get stronger to protect Zuleia and the party on future expeditions: "I went to school until I ran away. But, you know, the one thing I've always been able to rely on is that I have been strong enough to take care of myself... You bandit idiots. I kinda like y'all, and I kinda want y'all to not die."

When Sid challenged this—"You can't be protecting everyone either way. People sometimes don't want to be protected"—Sabrina responded: "I think it's important to choose your weight."

**On Zuleia**: Sabrina acknowledged that Zuleia "carries not just the weight of her own expectations, she carries the ones set for me too." The family has younger brothers (ages approximately 12 and 15).

*Quote: "Pigeonholing is what leads to people like me leaving the city."*
