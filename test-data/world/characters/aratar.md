# Aratar

The **Aratar**, also known as the **Eight**, or the **Holy Ones of Arda**, were the greatest of the Valar.

The Aratar were Manwë, Varda, Ulmo, Yavanna, Aulë, Mandos, Nienna, and Oromë. Melkor was removed from the order upon his rebellion. Although Tulkas was a Vala residing in Valinor, he was not counted among the Aratar.

The Aratar were also called the ***Máhani***.

==Etymology== 
***Aratar*** is a Quenya word that meant 'the Exalted'.
