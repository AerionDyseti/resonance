Prince of [Kenkuru](../../Locations/Locations%20DB/Kenkuru%201f9ca0aafe2181f2aa30f80ba3b9c582.md), and oath-sworn to [Karr Goldman](Karr%20Goldman%201f9ca0aafe2181f89086d9eed9dc46c8.md), he has served as Karr’s caretaker, guardian, and right-hand man for a long time. When Kenkuru was attacked by [The Spider Aeon](../../Lore/Lore%20DB/The%20Spider%20Aeon%20207ca0aafe21804d8267ecf3df4edec8.md) he was driven away from [Raknagal](../../Locations/Locations%20DB/Raknagal%201f9ca0aafe2181f18b58de0125bf07e2.md) and only recently returned. Is known to have some likely-romantic connections with [Krounli Greyemith](Krounli%20Greyemith%201f9ca0aafe218126a157d4ae32807032.md).
