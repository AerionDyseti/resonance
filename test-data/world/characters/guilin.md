# Guilin

**Guilin** was a noble Elf of Nargothrond and the father of Gelmir and Gwindor.

## Biography

Though little is known about him, Guilin was the father of Gelmir and Gwindor, both of whom played major roles in the First Age. Gelmir's death initiated the Nírnaeth Arnoediad, which resulted in the captivity and enslavement of Gwindor in Angband.
