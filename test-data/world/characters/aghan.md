# Aghan

**Aghan** was a dark-skinned man of the Drúedain tribes who lived in the First Age.

## Biography
He had a friend named Barach, who lived on the borders of the Forest of Brethil, two miles away from the nearest village. Aghan took to watching their house when Orcs started raiding nearby. When his brother was ill, he made a statue to watch the house. When a group of Orcs started burning Barach's house, a mysterious Drûg killed the Orcs and put out the fires, but was burned by the flames. Aghan suffered from blisters at the same time. The mysterious Drûg turned out to be the statue.
