## Description

Iamus is a blue-skinned tiefling with elegant black horns that curve up from his temples. Despite his gangly frame and somewhat awkward posture, he has a quiet nature and his warm, friendly smile makes people feel at ease.

## History

Born in Osiria's jungles, Iamus grew up working the land alongside his parents on their farm. His friendly nature and strong work ethic made him well-liked and he found that his heat tolerance was great even for a Tiefling, making him exceptionally suited for jungle farming. Though not scholarly, his practical knowledge of jungle flora earned respect from local farmers. After saving enough money, he bought his own land when the estate owner died. Now he grows tropical crops and is a familiar face at the local market, where he trades produce and shares farming advice.

## Relationships

His childhood friendship with [Zaelith](Zaelith%201f9ca0aafe2181358e4cebfa216edfa3.md) remains string despite their very different paths - Zaelith in politics and Iamus in farming. Iamus often provides a grounded perspective for Zaelith, and they often meet to exchange crops and stories.
