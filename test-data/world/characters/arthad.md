# Arthad

**Arthad** was an Edain man of the House of Bëor in the First Age.

## Biography

Arthad was a man of Dorthonion before the Dagor Bragollach. Afterwards, he was one of the XII Bëorings that lived in Dorthonion, following Barahir in defying Morgoth's forces. He was killed with the other outlaws at Tarn Aeluin as a result of Gorlim's betrayal.
