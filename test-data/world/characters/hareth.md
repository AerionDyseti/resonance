# Hareth

**Hareth** was an Edain woman of the House of Haleth.

## Biography
Hareth lived during the First Age of Middle-earth, and was the elder daughter of Halmir, the Chieftain of the Haladin, and sister of Haldir, Hundar, and Hiril. She married Galdor in FA 436 of Dor-lómin, Lord of the House of Hador, and bore him two sons, Húrin and Huor.

== Etymology == 
***Hareth*** was a Quenya word that meant 'Lady'.
