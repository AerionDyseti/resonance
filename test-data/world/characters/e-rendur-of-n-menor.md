# Eärendur of Númenor

**Eärendur** was a Númenórean nobleman of the royal House of Elros.

## Biography
He was the younger son of Tar-Amandil, the third King of Númenor. He had a son, Caliondo, who in turn had a son, Malantur. Malantur was apparently a possible heir to Tar-Aldarion, his cousin, but the law was changed such that Aldarion's daughter Ancalimë would be the heir.

## Etymology
The name ***Eärendur*** is Quenya for 'Servant of the Sea'.
