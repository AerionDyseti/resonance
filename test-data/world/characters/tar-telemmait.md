# Tar-Telemmaitë

**Tar-Telemmaitë** was the fifteenth King of Númenor.

## Biography

Tar-Telemmaitë succeeded his father, Tar-Ancalimon. During his reign, he bade his servants to seek as much mithril as could be found. He ruled Númenor for 140 years, and was 390 when he died. His daughter, Tar-Vanimeldë, succeeded him and became Númenor's third Ruling Queen.
