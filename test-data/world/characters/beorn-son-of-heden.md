# Beorn (son of Heden)

**Beorn** was a son of a Man named Heden, according to the early version of the legendarium in *The Book of Lost Tales*. who died either "in the siege" of his town or "in a great battle".

## Etymology
The name *Beorn* is in Old English. It means "warrior", but its original meaning was "bear".

The name *Veran* itself was changed from *Verus*, an earlier name.
