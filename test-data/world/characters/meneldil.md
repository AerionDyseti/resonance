# Meneldil

**Meneldil** was the third King of Gondor.

## Biography
Meneldil was born in Númenor a year before its fall.

## Etymology
The name ***Meneldil*** is a Quenya word meaning *Heaven-lover* or *Lover of the Heavens.*
