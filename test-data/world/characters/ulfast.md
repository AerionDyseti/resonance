# Ulfast

**Ulfast** was an Easterling of the First Age.

## Biography

Ulfast was one of the three sons of Ulfang. He followed his father in the Nírnaeth Arnoediad where they betrayed the host of Caranthir. He was killed by the sons of Bór for he and his father's treachery against Caranthir.
