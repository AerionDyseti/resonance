# Hunthor

**Hunthor** was an Edain of the House of Haleth, and was the only man to accompany Túrin when Túrin went to slay Glaurung.

## Biography

Hunthor was a kinsman of Brandir the Lame, and was known to have had a wife. His brother Manthor appears in "The Wanderings of Húrin" (Turin's father), after Túrin's death.

## Other versions of the legendarium
In earlier versions of the legendarium, Hunthor's name was **Torbath**.
