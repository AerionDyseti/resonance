# Emeldir

**Emeldir**, also known as **Emeldir the Manhearted**, was an Edain woman of the House of Bëor. She was the daughter of Beren who was the son of Belemir of the House of Bëor and Adanel of the House of Marach. She had two unnamed elder siblings, making her the third child of Beren (son of Belemir).

## Biography

Emeldir was the wife of Barahir, who was also of the House of Bëor through the line of Baran, the eldest son of Bëor the Old.

Her only grandchild and grandson was the Half-elf Dior, who later became the King of Doriath, and her great-grandchildren were the twins Eluréd and Elurín, and their younger sister Elwing.

## Etymology
***Emel*** means 'mother' in Sindarin.
