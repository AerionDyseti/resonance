# Gamil Zirak

**Gamil Zirak**, also known as **Zirak** and the **Old**, was a Dwarf smith who taught Telchar of Nogrod during the First Age.

## Biography
Little is known of Gamil Zirak's life, but it is known that they were a Dwarf smith of the Firebeards or Broadbeams In the *Narn i Chîn Húrin* that was written by Dírhaval, some of the "great wealth of weapons" amassed in Thingol's "deep armouries" of Menegroth were forged by Gamil Zirak and Telchar. These weapons included "metals wrought like fishes' mail" which shone under moonlight like water.

## Etymology
- Gamil Zirak* was a Khuzdûl name likely meaning "old spike" or "old tine", being comprised of the words *gamil* ("old; venerable") tine") from the root *ZRK*
