# Galadhrim Warriors

### *The Lord of the Rings* film trilogy

Most of them, including Haldir, fell in combat further into the night.

In J.R.R. Tolkien's books, Lórien could not spare any forces to help the other kingdoms against Sauron. Lórien barely survived three assaults from Dol Guldur, and so this move of the Elves did not happen.
