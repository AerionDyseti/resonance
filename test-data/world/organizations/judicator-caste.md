# Judicator Caste

Type: Political, Religious
Influence: National

## Overview

The judicial and law enforcement branch of Solaaran society. They interpret divine law, conduct trials, and ensure justice according to Solaaran principles.

## Core Purpose

Legal judgment and enforcement of divine law

## History

One of the founding Nine Castes, the Judicators serve as judges, investigators, and legal enforcers. Known for their unwavering commitment to order and justice.

**Founded:** 743 MR

**Status:** Active
