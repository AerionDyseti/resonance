# White Company

The **White Company** was the guard of the Princes of Ithilien during the Fourth Age.

## History
The company was created by King Elessar in  to settle the complex case of Beregond, as he had abandoned his post and spilled blood, yet saved Faramir from an early death upon doing so. Elessar exiled Beregond from Minas Tirith, but appointed him to be the first **Captain of the Guard** of Prince Faramir to dwell within the hills of Emyn Arnen in sight of Minas Tirith.

During the early Fourth Age, Borlas and two old servants retired to Pen-arduin from the '''Prince's guard**.
