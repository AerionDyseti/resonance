# Entertainer Caste

Type: Religious, Social
Influence: National

## Overview

Performers, musicians, storytellers, and those who bring joy and cultural richness to Solaaran society. They preserve traditions and lift spirits.

## Core Purpose

Joy, celebration, and cultural expression

## History

One of the Nine Castes, recognizing that joy and celebration are essential to a healthy society. They maintain cultural traditions and public morale.
