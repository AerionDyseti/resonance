# Naith of Lórien

The **Naith of Lórien**, also known as the **Gore**, was the heart of the Elven realm of Lothlórien, near Caras Galadhon.

The Naith was located in the angle between the rivers Silverlode and Anduin.

## History

The Fellowship of the Ring first encountered the sentinels of Lórien at the Naith, through which they were led to the Lord and Lady in Caras Galadhon. Here also Frodo Baggins first experienced the feeling of having entered a corner of the Elder Days.

## Etymology

- Naith* is a Sindarin word meaning "angle".
