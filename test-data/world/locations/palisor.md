# Palisor

**Palisor** was the name for the 'Midmost Region' of the 'Great Lands' of Arda (the future Middle-earth) described in  and . 

According to , Palisor lay between the Iron Mountains, the Misty Mountains, the Orocarni, and the Hither Lands with the Sea of Helcar in the center.

## History
Palisor is described as the place where the Elves first awoke, and was the location of a lake named Cuiviénen, the Water of Awakening. It was a place where great pinewood forests existed and murmured with strange noises in these early days. The area was long populated with Dark Elves, and would later evolve into the land of Rhûn, Rhovanion, and possibly Mordor and Khand.
