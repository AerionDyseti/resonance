# Kalimban

**Kalimban** was a region in the north of the Great Lands, in earlier versions of J.R.R. Tolkien's legendarium.

## History
The people of Kalimban, the *Kalimbardi*, were known as savage barbarians, hostile to the Elves and Men of Leithian.

## Etymology
The word *Kalimban* means "barbary" in Qenya.
