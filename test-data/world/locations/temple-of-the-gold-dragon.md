# Temple of the Gold Dragon

Category: Site
Location Description: A site dedicated to the Gold Dragon, associated with the realms of Logic, Order, and Purpose, linked to the Primordial Aeon and utilizing the Force damage type.

One of the [Sacred Temples of the Dragons](Sacred%20Temples%20of%20the%20Dragons%20207ca0aafe21809ab2d0fe511603479d.md), dedicated to the Gold Dragon and its realm of Logic, Order, and Purpose. Tied to the Primordial Aeon [Temnis](../../Lore/Lore%20DB/Temnis%20207ca0aafe21801daea5cebe2f909a48.md), and wields the Force damage type.
