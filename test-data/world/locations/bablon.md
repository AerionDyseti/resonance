# Bablon

**Bablon** was a city of Men in the Great Lands, according to the early version of the legendarium in *The Book of Lost Tales*. It was identified with the city of Babylon.

The city was mentioned in the tale of *the Fall of Gondolin* by Littleheart, where he compared its fall as even being worse than Bablon's.

## Etymology
The name *Bablon* is in Gnomish, but its meaning is unclear. However, it was probably a phonological imitation of *Babylon*.
