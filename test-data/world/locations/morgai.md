# Morgai

The **Morgai**, also known as the **Black Fence**, was a mountainous ridge in the Ephel Dúath (Mountains of Shadow) which formed a defensive inner wall in Mordor.

Between it and the Ephel Dúath lay a road which ran between Durthang and the Morgul Pass. Frodo Baggins and Samwise Gamgee followed this road north for a while after the most direct crossing of the Morgai was blocked by a Nazgûl.
