# Hyarmentir

**Hyarmentir** was a mountain southeast of Valinor in Aman.

## History

Hyarmentir was the highest mountain peak in southern Aman. Ruamōre was located south of it. Before they destroyed the Two Trees, Melkor and Ungoliant gazed upon the bliss of the Blessed Realm from one of its highest peak.

## Etymology

***Hyarmentir*** is a Quenya word that meant 'South Watch'.
