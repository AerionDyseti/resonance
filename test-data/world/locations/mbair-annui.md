# Mbair Annui

**Mbair Annui**, the **West-lands**, was a term used in relation to the north-western region of Middle-earth between the river Carnen and Belegaer, though sometimes it was used to refer to Eriador.

## History
The Teleri halted there for a time near a "Great River" during their initial journey westward.

## Etymology
- Mbair Annui* is a name in Sindarin meaning "Westlands", consisting of *mbair* ("lands"), a variation of *bâr* ("dwelling; house, home"), and *annui'' ("west").
