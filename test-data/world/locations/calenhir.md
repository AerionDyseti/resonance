# Calenhir

**Calenhir** was a small river located in Gondor. It was a tributary of the larger river Morthond, originating from Pinnath Gelin and flowing westward.

The river only appears in earlier versions of the map J.R.R. Tolkien drew for , and is mentioned only in . As such, its canonicity remains unclear.

## Etymology
***Calenhir*** appears to mean "green river" in Sindarin.
