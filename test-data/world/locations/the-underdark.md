# The Underdark

Category: Stratum
Location Description: The Underdark is a vast network of subterranean caverns beneath Enym, characterized by eternal darkness and illuminated by bioluminescent fungi. It hosts civilizations like Vrasillex, a Dro'adi city, and the Deep Mycelium, an ancient fungal cave network. Inhabitants include Dro'adi, Myconid colonies, and various adapted creatures.

## Overview

The Underdark is the vast network of subterranean caverns, tunnels, and ecosystems that exists beneath the surface world of Enym.

## Description

A realm of eternal darkness illuminated only by bioluminescent fungi, crystal formations, and magical light. The Underdark contains entire civilizations, ancient ruins, and ecosystems that have developed in isolation from the surface world.

## Notable Regions

- **Vrasillex**: A major Dro'adi city governed by thirteen matriarchal houses
- **Deep Mycelium**: Ancient fungal cave network beneath Vrasillex where time behaves strangely
- Trade routes connecting various Underdark settlements

## Inhabitants

- Dro'adi (Deep Elves)
- Myconid colonies
- Various adapted creatures
