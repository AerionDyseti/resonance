# Drúwaith Iaur

**Drúwaith Iaur** also known as **Old Púkel land** was a land west of Gondor between the river Isen and the White Mountains.

## History
Drúwaith Iaur was supposedly one of the homes to the remnants of the Drúedain or Woses, and perhaps other Men who had once lived in the White Mountains, but its population was never fully established by J.R.R. Tolkien.

## Etymology
***Drúwaith Iaur*** is Sindarin for 'Land of the old wild-folk'.
