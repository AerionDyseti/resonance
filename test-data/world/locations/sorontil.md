# Sorontil

**Sorontil**, also known as **Eagle-horn**, was a great mountain peak in the north of the Númenórean province of Forostar.

Sorontil rose up from the coasts of Forostar as sheer, tremendously steep cliffs. It was here that King Tar-Meneldur built his tower upon Sorontil.
