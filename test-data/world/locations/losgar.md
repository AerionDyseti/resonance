# Losgar

**Losgar** was a region located at the mouth of the Firth of Drengist in Middle-earth.

## History

Losgar was the place that Fëanor and his followers landed and where he ordered the burning of the Swanships that he stole from the Teleri of Alqualondë, stranding Fingolfin and his followers. Fingolfin observed this from Araman in Aman and realized that he had been betrayed, thus forcing him and his followers to undergo a long and hard trek across the Helcaraxë to Middle-earth.

## Etymology

***Losgar*** is a Sindarin word.
