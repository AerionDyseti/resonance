# Dáin's hall

'''Dáin's hall''' was the dwelling of Durin's Folk in the Grey Mountains, the name coming from the Dwarf-king Dáin I.

## History
Little is known about this hall save that in , Dáin was killed by a great Cold-Drake along with his son Frór at the entrance to the hall. A year later, most of Durin's Folk (though not all) abandoned the mountains, and left for Erebor and the Iron Hills lead by Thrór and Grór, respectively, Dáin's other two sons.

## Portrayal in adaptations
