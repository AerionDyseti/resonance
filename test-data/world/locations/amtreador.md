# Amtreador

Category: Nation
Contains: Galel Balenir (Galel%20Balenir%201f9ca0aafe2181f4821fe593358f3b13.md), Dragon's Den (Dragon's%20Den%201fcca0aafe2180e28c5cd0e83c06fd36.md), Threadbore Cabin (Threadbore%20Cabin%201fcca0aafe2180d6b17cc9e0e1222f33.md), Galel Anastrael (Galel%20Anastrael%20216ca0aafe218027b1c8da3e0a0161b3.md)
Part Of: Tyrre (Tyrre%201f9ca0aafe218151abacec629dcc4892.md)
Location Description: Ancient Elven forest kingdom home to both the Amtreadi (High Elves) and Liinadi (Wood Elves), serving as the political and magical center of Elven civilization. The kingdom has existed since approximately 6000 MR and survived the Great Sundering (~5200 MR) when High Elves nearly exterminated the Deep Elves, splitting the Elven peoples. Currently a theocracy led by Cais, Speaker of the Elves, operating under Anastrasael's (The Starfather's) guidance. Notable features include ancient elven forests, magical academies, the Hall of Secrets (political and knowledge repository), celestial observatories for divine guidance, and sacred sites marking important historical locations.

Elven Forest and home of the Liinadi (wood elves) and the Amtreadi (high elves).
