# Temple of the Silver Dragon

Category: Site
Location Description: A site dedicated to the Silver Dragon, associated with the element of Cold and the Elemental Aeon, focusing on themes of reflection, self-actualization, and insight.

One of the [Sacred Temples of the Dragons](Sacred%20Temples%20of%20the%20Dragons%20207ca0aafe21809ab2d0fe511603479d.md), dedicated to the Silver Dragon and its corresponding element of Cold. Tied to the Elemental Aeon [Karasi](../../Lore/Lore%20DB/Karasi%20207ca0aafe21805fb42afd2cd79ca998.md) , and the concepts of reflection, self-actualization, and insight.
