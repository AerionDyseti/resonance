# Forlindon

**Forlindon** was the northern part of the Elvish Realm of Lindon, which was separated from Harlindon by the Gulf of Lune.

The Elven-haven and harbor of Forlond lay on the southern coast of the region and was ruled by the Elven King Gil-galad.

Like the rest of Lindon it was a remnant of the region of  Beleriand that sank under the Great Sea after the War of Wrath, at the end of the First Age.

## Etymology

The name ***Forlindon*** possibly means "North Lindon", from the Quenya word *formen* and Sindarin words *for, forn, forod* ("north") and Lindon.
