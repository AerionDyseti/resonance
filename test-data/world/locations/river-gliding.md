# River Gliding

The **River Gliding**, also known as the **Gliding Water**, was a river that flowed through Kôrtirion, a city on Tol Eressëa.

According to the poem *The Trees of Kortirion*, the river is described as flowing westward from Kôrtirion, passing through meadows before reaching the sea. The River Gliding is surrounded by elms and is part of the idyllic landscape of Tol Eressëa.
