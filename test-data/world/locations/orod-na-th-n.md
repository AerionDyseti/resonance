# Orod-na-Thôn

**Orod-na-Thôn**, also known as the **Foen**, and high mountain that was located in the midst of Dorthonion.

## History
Sometime in the Elder Days, Treebeard climbed up Orod-na-Thôn in Dorthonion during a snowy winter in Beleriand. Treebeard mentioned his time on Orod-na-Thôn in a song he sang as he took Meriadoc Brandybuck and Peregrin Took to Wellinghall in Fangorn Forest. associated with, marked with"), and *thôn* ("pine-tree"
