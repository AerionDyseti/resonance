# Arvernien

**Arvernien** was a region located in southern Beleriand west of the Mouths of Sirion.

==History== 

The first rulers of the region of Arvernien were Tuor and Idril, who were refugees from Gondolin. Their son Eärendil later married Elwing, Dior's daughter and survivor of the Second Sack of Doriath. Elros and Elrond, the sons of Eärendil and Elwing, were born in Arvernien.

## Etymology

***Arvernien*** was a Sindarin name.
