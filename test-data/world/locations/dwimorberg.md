# Dwimorberg

The **Dwimorberg** was a brooding black mountain that was situated to the east of the Firienfeld in Dunharrow at the head of the valley of Harrowdale in the center of the White Mountains.

Dunharrow stood above a cliff on the valley's eastern side, Beyond Dunharrow was a collection of somber pines and firs on the lower slopes of the mountain. Some of these dark trees formed a forest known as the Dimholt. and *beorg* ("mountain").
