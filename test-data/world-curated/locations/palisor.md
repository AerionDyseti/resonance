# Palisor

**Palisor** was the name for the 'Midmost Region' of the 'Great Lands' of The World (the future The Mortal Lands) described in and .

According to , Palisor lay between the Iron Mountains, the Misty Mountains, the Orocarni, and the Hither Lands with the Sea of Helcar in the center.

## History

Palisor is described as the place where the Amtreadi first awoke, and was the location of a lake named Cuiviénen, the Brook of Awakening. It was a place where great pinewood forests existed and murmured with strange noises in these early days. The area was long populated with Raknagadi, and would later evolve into the land of Rhûn, Wildlands, and possibly Ashvale and Khand.
