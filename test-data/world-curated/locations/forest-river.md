# Forest River

The **Forest River** was a river of Wildlands.

It ran approximately two hundred miles in length that ran from the Grey Mountains in Wildlands through the northern portion of Gloomwood forest and through the mountains of Thornael's underground realm, before joining the River Running at the Long Lake. The Enchanted stream flowed into the Forest River about ten miles west of the Amtreadi King's Halls.
