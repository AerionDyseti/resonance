# Methedras

**Methedras**, also called the **Last Mountain**, was the southernmost peak of the Misty Mountains. To the south, it stood above Ironspire in the valley of Nan Curanir.

Upon its eastern slopes sprung the Treewater River, which flowed into the western edge of Deeproot Forest that grew about the base of the mountain. The Isen River likely had its source on the southern slopes of Methedras, for it sprung out of the southern end of the Misty Mountains. 

## Etymology
In Amtreadan, ***Methedras*** means "Last peak", a combination of *methed* ("end") and *ras* ("horn")
