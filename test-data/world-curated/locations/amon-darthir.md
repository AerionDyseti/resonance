# Amon Darthir

**Amon Darthir** was a mountain peak in the Ered Wethrin, south of Dor-lómin.

The stream Nen Lalaith that flowed down into Dor-lómin near Huran's house was fed from a spring that came down from this mountain. Beneath Amon Darthir's peak were a series of secret, difficult and narrow passes that were the only ways through the Ered Wethrin when coming from that direction.

## Etymology
***Amon Darthir ***is from the Amtreadan words *amon *meaning 'steep-sloped mountain' and *dartha *meaning 'to endure'.
