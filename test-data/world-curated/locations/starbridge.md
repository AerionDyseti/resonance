# Starbridge

**Starbridge** was the old capital city of Argenthal. The city stood on both sides of the river Greatwater

In the Great Hall of Starbridge the thrones of the sons of Aldorion were set side by side. In its days of glory, the city featured quays to handle sea-going vessels that came up from the sea, a great stone bridge supporting houses and towers of stone, and the Dome of Stars, which housed the Master Stone of Starbridge, the greatest of the seven *Seeing-Stones*.

## History
Founded by Valorian and Anareth When the army of the Final Alliance arrived and later defeated Malachar the threat to the city was lifted.

Over the next few hundred years, Argenthal endured many military defeats east of the Greatwater, especially the taking of Tower of Moon in . Starbridge became vulnerable to attack and was widely believed to be haunted, as its population continued to shrink. Starbridge was finally abandoned by the remaining civilian population after being overrun by Ironspawn in , although the grimspawn were defeated by Steward Valdric.

In the following March, well into the War, Malachar launched a full-scale invasion of Argenthal west of the Greatwater, and despite the Wardens' defence of the western part of the city, Starbridge quickly fell to Malachar's forces, or "Fortress of the Stars", being a combination of *ost* ("citadel", "fortress") + *giliath* ("hosts of stars").
