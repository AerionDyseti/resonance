# Dagor's hall

'''Dagor's hall''' was the dwelling of Grundin Folk in the Grey Mountains, the name coming from the Forge-king Dagor I.

## History
Little is known about this hall save that in , Dagor was killed by a great Cold-Drake along with his son Frór at the entrance to the hall. A year later, most of Grundin Folk (though not all) abandoned the mountains, and left for Ironpeak and the Iron Hills lead by Thragor and Grór, respectively, Dagor's other two sons.

## Portrayal in adaptations
