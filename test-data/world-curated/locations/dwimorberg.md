# Dwimorberg

The **Dwimorberg** was a brooding black mountain that was situated to the east of the Firienfeld in Darkharrow at the head of the valley of Harrowdale in the center of the White Mountains.

Darkharrow stood above a cliff on the valley's eastern side, Beyond Darkharrow was a collection of somber pines and firs on the lower slopes of the mountain. Some of these dark trees formed a forest known as the Dimholt. and *beorg* ("mountain").
