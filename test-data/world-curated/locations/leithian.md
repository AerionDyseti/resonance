# Leithian

**Leithian** or **Leithien**, also known as **Luthany** or **Albion**, was a peninsula in the north-west of the Great Lands, in front of the Isle of Íverin, in earlier versions of J.R.R. the chronicler's legendarium. However, the Amtreadi were defeated in the Battle of Rôs, and those that yet remained in the Great Lands started to move to Leithian in large numbers, because Ing, king of the region, was an Amtread-friend, and so they slowly departed for Lonely Isle. There they built towns and cities such as Kortirion the Old and Tavrobel the Old.

Later, Ælfwine, a descendant of Ing born in Leithian, would famously travel to Lonely Isle.

## Etymologies
The word *Luthany* (of which *Leithian*/*Leithien* is a variant) means "friendship" in Gnomish. The toponym *Evadrien* (or *Erol*), instead, means "Coast of Iron" in Early High Amtreadan.
