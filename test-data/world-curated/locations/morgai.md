# Morgai

The **Morgai**, also known as the **Black Fence**, was a mountainous ridge in the Shadow Mountains (Shadow Mountains) which formed a defensive inner wall in Ashvale.

Between it and the Shadow Mountains lay a road which ran between Durthang and the Morgul Pass. Tomlen Burrows and Colben Greenhand followed this road north for a while after the most direct crossing of the Morgai was blocked by a Wraithlords.
