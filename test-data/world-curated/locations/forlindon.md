# Forlindon

**Forlindon** was the northern part of the Amtreadan Realm of Westshore, which was separated from Harlindon by the Gulf of Lune.

The Amtreadi-haven and harbor of Forlond lay on the southern coast of the region and was ruled by the Amtreadi King Astorion.

Like the rest of Westshore it was a remnant of the region of  Westlands that sank under the Great Sea after the War of Wrath, at the end of the First Era.

## Etymology

The name ***Forlindon*** possibly means "North Westshore", from the High Amtreadan word *formen* and Amtreadan words *for, forn, forod* ("north") and Westshore.
