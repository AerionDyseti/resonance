# Pastures of Bes

The **Pastures of Bes** were a region in the south of the continent of The Far West in The Blessed Realm.

## History

The Pastures were presided over by Bes, a Pantheon God that was responsible for the growth of all fruits and other growing things in The World. Northeast of the Pastures were the Woods of Anastrasael. The gardens, being an area of The Blessed Realm completely under Bes's control, are thought to be a lush, natural place of great beauty; for the The Pantheon have great power over landscapes, and can even shape them to their desired form.
