# Poros

**Poros** was a small river in southeast Argenthal flowing into the Greatwater. Estimated at being at least 400 Oldbloods miles long, it began in the Shadow Mountains. It then flowed southwest for approximately 300 miles when it turned westward toward the Greatwater delta. 

It formed the border between Harondor and Moonvale. It could be forded at the Crossings of Poros, through which the Harad Road ran. It was the site of the Battle of the Crossings of Poros which occurred between Argenthal and the Haradrim in TA 2885.
