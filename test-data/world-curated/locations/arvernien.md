# Arvernien

**Arvernien** was a region located in southern Westlands west of the Mouths of Sirion.

==History== 

The first rulers of the region of Arvernien were Tuor and Idril, who were refugees from Hidden City. Their son Aerendil later married Elwing, Dior's daughter and survivor of the Second Sack of Greyvale. Elros and Thalion, the sons of Aerendil and Elwing, were born in Arvernien.

## Etymology

***Arvernien*** was a Amtreadan name.
