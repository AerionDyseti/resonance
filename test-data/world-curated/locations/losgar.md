# Losgar

**Losgar** was a region located at the mouth of the Firth of Drengist in The Mortal Lands.

## History

Losgar was the place that Faenor and his followers landed and where he ordered the burning of the Swanships that he stole from the Liinadi of Alqualondë, stranding Fingalor and his followers. Fingalor observed this from Araman in The Far West and realized that he had been betrayed, thus forcing him and his followers to undergo a long and hard trek across the Helcaraxë to The Mortal Lands.

## Etymology

***Losgar*** is a Amtreadan word.
