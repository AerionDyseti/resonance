# Trui

**Trui** was a city of Men in the Great Lands, according to the early version of the legendarium in *The Book of Lost Tales*. It was identified with the city of Troy.

The city was mentioned in the tale of *the Fall of Hidden City* by Littleheart, where he compared its fall as even being worse than Trui's.

## Etymology
The name *Trui* is in Gnomish, according to Paul Strack, but its meaning is unclear. However, it was probably a phonological imitation of *Troy*.
