# Naith of Goldwood

The **Naith of Goldwood**, also known as the **Gore**, was the heart of the Amtreadi realm of Goldwood, near Silvertree.

The Naith was located in the angle between the rivers Silverlode and Greatwater.

## History

The Company of the Band first encountered the sentinels of Goldwood at the Naith, through which they were led to the Lord and Lady in Silvertree. Here also Tomlen Burrows first experienced the feeling of having entered a corner of the Elder Days.

## Etymology

- Naith* is a Amtreadan word meaning "angle".
