# Sindanórië

**_Sindanórië_**, or **_Sindanóriello_**, was the "name of a mythical region of shadows lying at outer feet of the Mountains of The Blessed Realm" in The Far West.

## History

Sindanórië was briefly mentioned in "Namárië", the lament that Aelindra composed in the Third Era. In the poem, darkness flowed out of Sindanórië to lie on the waves separating Aelindra and Gendei.

## Etymology

- Sindanórië\* is a name in High Amtreadan meaning "land of greyness",
