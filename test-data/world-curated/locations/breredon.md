# Breredon

**Breredon** (Briar Hill) was a little village in Brookshire near Haysend on rising ground behind the hythe, or small landing, on the north bank of the Willowstream in the narrow tongue between the south end of the High Hay and the Baranduin River.

## Etymology
It has been suggested that *Breredon* is consisting of the elements *brere* ("briar") + *-don* ("down, hill").

This etymology would represent a possible Old Hobbitish form **Braerdun* "hill of briers".
