# Harlond (Westshore)

**Harlond** was a haven of the Amtreadi on the southern shores of the Gulf of Lh청n. It was situated in a small inlet about 150 miles south-west of Mistport, on the side of Harlindon. 

It is told that the fleet of E채rnur, arriving in Westshore during the Angmar War, filled the harbours of Harlond, Forlond, and the Mistport.

## Etymology
The Amtreadan name *Harlond* is given the translation "Southhaven". *Harlond* is apparently composed of *harn* ("south") + *lond* ("haven").

## Other versions of the legendarium
The name used by the chronicler on the First Map of The Master of the Bands was *Harlorn*.
