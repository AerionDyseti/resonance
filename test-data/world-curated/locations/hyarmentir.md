# Hyarmentir

**Hyarmentir** was a mountain southeast of The Blessed Realm in The Far West.

## History

Hyarmentir was the highest mountain peak in southern The Far West. Ruamōre was located south of it. Before they destroyed the Two Trees, Tenebros and The Webmother gazed upon the bliss of the Blessed Realm from one of its highest peak.

## Etymology

***Hyarmentir*** is a High Amtreadan word that meant 'South Watch'.
