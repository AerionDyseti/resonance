# Fanuidhol

**Fanuidhol** was a mountain in the Misty Mountains, and was adjacent to Redhorn and Celebdil, and were some of the greatest peaks in the Misty Mountains. 

Fanuidhol, Redhorn, and Celebdil provided the ceiling of the ancient Forgekin city of Shadowdeep.

==Etymology== 

- Fanuidhol* is a Amtreadan name with "fanui-" meaning [Cloudy], and "-dhol' meaning [helm]. Its name in Forgespeech was **Bundushathûr**, meaning "**Cloudyhead**".
