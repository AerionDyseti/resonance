# Forlond

**Forlond** was a harbour that lay on the northern shore of the Gulf of Lune, in the Amtreadan land of Forlindon. It had a counterpart on the southern shore: the Harlond.

Forlond was located at the mouth of an unnamed river that traversed Forlindon, some two hundred miles to the northwest of the Mistport.

When the ships of the Solaaran general EÃ¤rnur arrived in Westshore during the Angmar War, it is told that they filled the Forlond, Harlond and the Mistport.

## Etymology
According to Robert Foster, *Forlond* is a Amtreadan name meaning "north-haven".

## Other versions of the legendarium
The name used by the chronicler on the First Map of The Master of the Bands was *Forlorn*.
