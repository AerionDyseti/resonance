# Erui

The river **Erui** arose in the White Mountains. It was not counted amongst the Seven Rivers of Argenthal.

The river began near Mount Mindolluin in the White Mountains, flowing southward through the province of Flowerdale, where it met the Greatwater about one hundred Oldbloods miles south of Silverspire.

During the Kin-strife of Argenthal, the Battle of the Crossings of Erui was fought at the Crossings that lay on the way to Pelargir.
