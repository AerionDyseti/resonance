# Calembel

**Calembel** was a town in the region of Lamedon in northern Argenthal. It was located on a small hill near the road between Erech and Pelargir, near the fords of the river Ciril.

## History

On 9 March , Aldric, the Grey Company and the Army of Ghosts departed from Erech, crossed the fords over the river Ciril and arrived at Calembel. The fords over the river Ciril and the town of Calembel were deserted, because many men had gone to fight in the War of the Binding and the remaining population had fled to the hills after they heard a rumour that the King of the Ghosts was coming.
