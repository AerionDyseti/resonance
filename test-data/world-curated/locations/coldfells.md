# Coldfells

The **Coldfells** were a hilly region located to the north of Galel Anastrael.

Aldric II's grandfather Arador was captured in the Coldfells and slain by Hill-stonelurks.

## In adaptations

In , the Coldfells are located in the southern portion of the Ettenmoors. The region contains a keep called Tirith Rhaw that can be controlled by either the forces of Angmar or the Free Peoples of The Mortal Lands.
