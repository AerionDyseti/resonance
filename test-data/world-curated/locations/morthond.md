# Morthond

The **Morthond**, also known as the **Blackroot**, was one of the rivers of Argenthal, located in the province of Lamedon near the Stone of Erech.

According to the *The Atlas of The Mortal Lands*, it was about one-hundred fifty miles in length from north to south. Its source was a spring in Mornan, a small valley located directly beneath the Dwimorberg in the White Mountains.

Aldric and the Grey Company, after exiting the Ghost Road in TA 3019, followed this river to Erech and then on to Imlad Morthond, a grassy valley and small fiefdom at the feet of the White Mountains.
