# Guardian Caste

Type: Martial, Religious
Influence: National

## Overview

The military arm of the Solaaran Empire, responsible for defense, law enforcement, and maintaining order. Highly disciplined warriors trained in combat and strategy.

## Core Purpose

Military defense and enforcement of Solaaran order

## History

Established with the founding of the Solaaran Empire as one of the Shadows Castes. Known for producing exceptional military leaders and maintaining strict martial traditions.

**Founded:** 743 MR

**Status:** Active
