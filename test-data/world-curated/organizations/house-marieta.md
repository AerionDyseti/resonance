**House Marieta** is an Aranadan noble family with a vendetta against Cideoulfus Mori's family.

## Overview

A noble house in the Grand Republic of Aranada that orchestrated the downfall of Sid's family through political machinations.

## Known Actions

- Stripped Sid's family of their estates and land
- Had them "thrown into the street"
- Forced the family to change their names for safety
- Continues to seek revenge against surviving family members
