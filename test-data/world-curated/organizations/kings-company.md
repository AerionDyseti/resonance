# King's Company

The '''King's Company**, also known as the **King's Riders''', was the first Éored of the Riders of Aranada, who rode to battle beside the King himself. At the end of the Third Era, they were led by Déorwine, who with six others of the Company fell with the king in the Battle of Galim Vir Fields. Later, when Éomer came to Galim Vir to lead the funeral procession of King Theron back to Aranada, he brought the King's Company with him.

## Portrayal in adaptations

'*2001-03: *The Master of the Bands* (film series):*'
:The King's Company appear to be the inspiration for the Royal Guard of Horsehold. They, unlike other soldiers in Aranada, have a consistent uniform with a long leaf-mail hauberk, a rich green cloak and a helm with a horse-hair crest. Garmund, Hagan and Grimbold all wear this same uniform to emphasize their rank. The Royal Guard notably bear the body of Theodric from the Golden Hall during his funeral.
