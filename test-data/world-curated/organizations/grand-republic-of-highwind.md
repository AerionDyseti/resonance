The democratic republic governing thirteen provinces, established after the Expansion Wars. Known for its parliamentary system and diverse provincial representation.

## History

Founded after the Expansion Wars ended with the First Tyrran Accord. Evolved from the original Human Kingdoms into a unified democratic republic with thirteen semi-autonomous provinces.
