# Healer Caste

Type: Religious, Social
Influence: National

## Overview

Medical practitioners, healers, and caretakers responsible for the physical and spiritual wellbeing of all citizens. They blend divine healing with practical medicine.

## Core Purpose

Medicine, healing, and preservation of life

## History

One of the Shadows Castes, dedicated to preserving and restoring life. They serve all castes equally and are revered for their compassion and skill.
