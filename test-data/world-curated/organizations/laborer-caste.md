# Laborer Caste

Type: Economic, Social
Influence: National

## Overview

The foundation of Solaaran society, responsible for construction, farming, basic crafts, and manual labor. Often viewed as humble but essential to the empire's function.

## Core Purpose

Physical work and craftsmanship that sustains society

## History

One of the Shadows Castes, representing the dignity of honest work. Despite being considered lower in the social hierarchy, they are respected for their essential contributions.

**Founded:** 743 MR

**Status:** Active
