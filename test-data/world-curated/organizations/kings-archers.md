# King's Archers

The '''King's Archers''' were the feared archers of the armies of Oldshore during the late Second Era.

## History
At the time when the Oldbloods carved out dominions and colonies within The Mortal Lands, they bore bows crafted from hollow steel and their black-feathered arrows were famously one ell long (that is, 1.143 m, or nearly four feet). It is likely that the King's Archers perished during the Downfall of Oldshore.
