# Knights of Ma’at

Type: Martial, Religious
Headquarters: Lux (../../Locations/Locations%20DB/Lux%201f9ca0aafe21817cba5edf7747a6c74e.md)

Four Knights that aided the original Queen of Solaarus, [Isis of Sol](../../Characters/Characters%20DB/Isis%20of%20Sol%20216ca0aafe2180789f8cdc758c82dd58.md) in clearing and preparing the city of [Lux](../../Locations/Locations%20DB/Lux%201f9ca0aafe21817cba5edf7747a6c74e.md) during the foudning of the [Solaaran Empire](Solaaran%20Empire%201f9ca0aafe218141b3bdf4195bb39bc8.md).

They wielded powerful weapons, Vindex Ma’at, (Holy Cleansing in Solaaran), which were rumored to be blades gifted to them by angelic soldiers loyal to [Sol](../../Lore/Lore%20DB/Sol%20207ca0aafe218053bc26cd98c27a6a61.md).
