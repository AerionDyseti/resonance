# Haldar

**Haldar** (born FA 341 - died FA 375) was the son of Haldad, leader of the Haladin (House of Haleth), twin brother to Haleth and the father of Haldan. He came to Westlands with his family and his people in the year 420.

He died in the Battle of the Gelion, attempting to retrieve the body of his father, who was slain while trying to lead a sortie against a siege of Grimspawn.
