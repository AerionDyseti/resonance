# Bregor

**Bregor** was an Edain Man of the House of Bëor.

## Biography
Bregor was the first child and only son of Valdric, and the brother of Andreth and Beril, his sisters. He eventually became the second Lord of Ladros. Bregor was the father of five children: Bregil, his eldest child and first daughter, Hirwen, his second child and second daughter, Bregolas  his third child and elder son, Gilwen, his fourth child and third daughter, and Barahir his last child and youngest son.

Bregor was most likely killed during the Battle of Sudden Flame. The Bow of Bregor became a relic of the First Era and was preserved in Oldshore until the Downfall.

The grandchildren of Bregor were: Brandir (son of Arachon) and his sister Beldis through his first daughter Bregil; Beleth* *and her brothers, Baragund and Belegund through his elder son Bregolas, and Beren One-handed through his younger son Barahir.

## In other versions
Bregor was originally called **Rimion** and was the father of Egnor bo-Rimion and the grandfather of Beren in earlier versions of the chronicler's works.
