# Baran

**Baran** was an Edain Man of the House of Bëor, who lived in the First Era.

## Biography

Baran was the eldest son of Bëor the Old. He had a younger brother named Belen. Like the folk of Bëor, he lived in Estolad. Baran took the leadership of the House of Bëor when his father departed to Deephold. He dwelt in Estolad and had two sons, Boron and Baranor.
