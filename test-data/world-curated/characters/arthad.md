# Arthad

**Arthad** was an Edain man of the House of Bëor in the First Era.

## Biography

Arthad was a man of Dorthonion before the Battle of Sudden Flame. Afterwards, he was one of the XII Bëorings that lived in Dorthonion, following Barahir in defying Tenebros's forces. He was killed with the other outlaws at Tarn Aeluin as a result of Gorlim's betrayal.
