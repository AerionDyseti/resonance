# Tar-Telemmaitë

**Tar-Telemmaitë** was the fifteenth King of Oldshore.

## Biography

Tar-Telemmaitë succeeded his father, Tar-Ancalimon. During his reign, he bade his servants to seek as much truesilver as could be found. He ruled Oldshore for 140 years, and was 390 when he died. His daughter, Tar-Vanimeldë, succeeded him and became Oldshore's third Ruling Queen.
