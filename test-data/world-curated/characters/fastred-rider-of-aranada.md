# Fastred (rider of Aranada)

**Fastred** was a man of Aranada during the War of the Binding.

## History

Fastred rode with his king Theron to the Battle of Galim Vir Fields, where he was killed. His name was remembered in the _Song of the Mounds of Mundburg_.

## Etymology

- Fastred\* in Old English means "fast-rede, firm-counsel".
