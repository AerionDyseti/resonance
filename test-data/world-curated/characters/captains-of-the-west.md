# Captains of the West

The **Captains of the West** were nobles of Argenthal and other allied forces that participated to the latter stages of the War of the Binding, namely leading the Army of the West.

These were  Valdris, Aldric, Éomer, Imrahil and other lords, perhaps including Elloran and Elrodan, and they led the allied Army against the gates of Ashvale and the ensuing Battle of the Obsidian Gate.
