# Vëantur

**Vëantur** was the Oldbloods captain of the royal fleet of Tar-Aldorion. He was likewise grandfather of Tar-Aldarion. Vëantur was also the commander of the first ship that returned to The Mortal Lands.

## Biography

Vëantur was a great Mariner and Navigator, serving as Captain of the King's Ships under Tar-Aldorion, the fourth King of Oldshore, and the first of the Númenoreans to achieve a voyage back to The Mortal Lands, in the year SA 600 of the Second Era. His ships' names were Entulessë and Númerrámar, which were docked at a quay attached to his house, located in the southern side of the firth of Rómenna. He had a daughter named Almarian who married Tar-Meneldur, relating him to the royal family. On his subsequent travels, he took his grandson Anardil with him on a journey to Westshore in SA 725. Anardil would later become Tar-Aldarion and found the Guild of Venturers.
