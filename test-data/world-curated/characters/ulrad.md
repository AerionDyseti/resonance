# Ulrad

**Ulrad** was one of the Men in the band of outlaws that was led by Turan Turambar. He was a friend of the group's former leader, Forweg, whom Turan had killed.  

Mîm the Petty-forgekin held Ulrad partly responsible for his son Khîm's death because Ulrad was one of the men that bound him.

Ulrad's later fate is untold.
