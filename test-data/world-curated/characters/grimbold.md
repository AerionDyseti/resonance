# Grimbold

**Grimbold** was a rider of Aranada, who dwelt in Grimslade. He fought in the Battles of the Fords of Iron, and fell in the Battle of Galim Vir Fields.

## History

Grimbold was a valiant captain under the Second Marshal. He was instrumental in the defence of the Fords of Iron; after his Marshal Theodric fell, he managed to hold the eyot in the river until Elfhelm arrived. Soon after, the troops of Ironspire left; their mission was over with the death of the Marshal.

Grimbold and his company were scattered after the Second Battle of the Fords of Iron, though most of his troops survived. When Valdris arrived, he sent Elfhelm to protect Horsehold and Grimbold to join Erikbrand, who was making his way to the Thornwall in an attempt to lift the siege.

After the victory at Thornwall Keep and the lighting of the warning beacons of Argenthal, Theron had to quickly reorganize his troops. The elderly Erikbrand commanded the defence of Aranada, while Grimbold was elected to take over command of the muster of the Westmark based on the valour he had shown at earlier battles. At the battle, Grimbold fought on the eastern side of the Galim Vir Fields, where he was slain. He was remembered in the _Song of the Mounds of Mundburg_ as "doughty".
