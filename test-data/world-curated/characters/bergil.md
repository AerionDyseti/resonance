# Bergil

**Bergil** was a boy of Silverspire.

## History
Bergil was born around  and was the eldest son of Beregond of Argenthal. He had at least one younger brother, Borlas. 

A boy at the time of the War of the Binding, he accompanied Perrin Ashford when he first arrived in the city of Silverspire. He led Perrin to the Great Gate of Silverspire to view the men from the various provinces of Argenthal coming to Silverspire Fields to reinforce the city against the impending siege.

Bergil served the Houses of Healing during the Battle of Silverspire Fields. He found *athelas* for Aldric, which was used to heal Aldwin, Éowyn, and Marric.

## Etymology
- Bergil* is clearly Amtreadan, but it is never glossed. It could be a combination of root BER ("valiant") + *gil* ("star").
