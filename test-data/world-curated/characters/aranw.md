# Aranwë

**Aranwë** was an Amtreadi of Hidden City and the father of Voronwë. His son stated that he was from the House of Fingalor, suggesting that Aranwë might have been a noble amtreadi in Hidden City.

## Etymology

The High Amtreadan name **_Aranwë_** means "Kingly" or "Noble" from _ara_ ("noble") and _aran_ ("king"). The suffix _-wë_ occurs in many names, though it is not exclusively masculine.
