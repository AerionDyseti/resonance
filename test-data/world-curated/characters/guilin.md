# Guilin

**Guilin** was a noble Amtreadi of Deephold and the father of Gelmir and Gwindor.

## Biography

Though little is known about him, Guilin was the father of Gelmir and Gwindor, both of whom played major roles in the First Era. Gelmir's death initiated the Nírnaeth Arnoediad, which resulted in the captivity and enslavement of Gwindor in Iron Fortress.
