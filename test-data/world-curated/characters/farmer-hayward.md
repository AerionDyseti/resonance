# Farmer Hayward

**Tolman "Tom" Hayward**, also known as **Farmer Hayward**, (Third Era  – Fourth Era , aged 99 years) was a Shirefolk of the Shirelands.

## History
Old Tom was a renowned farmer living on Bybrook's South Lane. He was married to Lily Brown, and they had five children. Young Tom was the eldest, and his siblings were Lily, Jolly, Nick and Nibs.

Farmer Hayward played an important part in the Battle of Bybrook, where he rallied many shirefolk to fight Sharkin's men. In early phases of writing Appendix F, Farmer Hayward's original Common Tongue name was given as *Tomacca Lothran* or (earlier) *Tomakli Lothron*.
