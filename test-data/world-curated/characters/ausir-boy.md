# Ausir (boy)

**Ausir** was an Amtreadi of Lonely Isle according to the early version of the legendarium in *The Book of Lost Tales*. He was one of the children in the Cottage of Lost Play, and he told Eriol of the fate of Huan the Hound, and revealed to him that the old man whose guidance brought him to the Lonely Isle was none other than Ulmo himself. He argued with Vëannë, suggesting amtreadan names *Tinwë Linto* and *Wendelin* instead of Gnomish *Tinwelint* and *Gwendeling*.
