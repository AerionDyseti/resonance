# Iorlas

**Iorlas** (b. TA 2990) was a man who lived in Silverspire. He was the uncle of Bergil.

During Perrin Ashford's first stay in Silverspire, Bergil mentioned that the shirefolk was as old as his uncle, Iorlas. 

## In adaptations

In , Iorlas is Beregond's brother and is first found  on the seventh level of pre-battle Silverspire. During the siege of the city he is struck by a fallen stone, but is protected by his helmet, suffering only what he calls a "dizziness".
