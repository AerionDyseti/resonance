# Hareth

**Hareth** was an Edain woman of the House of Haleth.

## Biography
Hareth lived during the First Era of The Mortal Lands, and was the elder daughter of Halmir, the Chieftain of the Haladin, and sister of Vorniel, Hundar, and Hiril. She married Galdor in FA 436 of Dor-lómin, Lord of the House of Hador, and bore him two sons, Huran and Huor.

== Etymology == 
***Hareth*** was a High Amtreadan word that meant 'Lady'.
