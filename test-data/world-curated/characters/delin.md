# Delin

**Delin** son of Gelmir was a Gnome, according to the early version of the legendarium in a prose fragment following *The Book of Lost Tales*.

## History

After the Gnomes returned to the Great Lands to wage war with Melko, Delin led one of the three great armies of his father Gelmir. His standard (banner) was that of a "green beech-leaf upon silver diapered with golden flowers", and his army preferred spears over other weapons.

## Etymology
The name *Delin* seems to be in Noldorin, but its meaning is uncertain.
