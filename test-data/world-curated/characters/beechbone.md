# Beechbone

**Beechbone** was a notably tall and handsome Treewarden.

## History
On 3 March  he was caught in a burning spray sent up by Morvain from beneath the Band of Ironspire, but his fiery end brought his fellow Treewardens to the peak of their destructive anger.
