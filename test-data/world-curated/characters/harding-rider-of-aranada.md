# Harding (rider of Aranada)

**Harding** was a man of Aranada during the War of the Binding.

## History

Harding fought and died in the Battle of Galim Vir Fields. His name is remembered in the poem Song of the Mounds of Mundburg.

## Etymology

Old English _hearding_ means "hard one, strong one".

## Portrayal in adaptations

'*2012: *The Master of the Bands Online: Riders of Aranada*:*'
:Harding is the "Aldor of the Eastemnet", governing all of East Aranada on behalf of the King. He dwells in the village of "Harwick", the main settlement of the Downs, of which he is also the Reeve. His son "Léodwig" along with Wídfara had disappeared in the remote province of "Wildermore" and Harding sends the player and his companions Gléowine and Horn to look for them. The player finds both wounded but alive and they eventually return to Harwick. Harding later dies on the Galim Vir Fields after slaying many foes.
