# Aratar

The **Aratar**, also known as the **Eight**, or the **Holy Ones of The World**, were the greatest of the The Pantheon.

The Aratar were Manwë, Varda, Ulmo, Yavanna, Aulë, Mandos, Nienna, and Oromë. Tenebros was removed from the order upon his rebellion. Although Tulkas was a Pantheon God residing in The Blessed Realm, he was not counted among the Aratar.

The Aratar were also called the ***Máhani***.

==Etymology== 
***Aratar*** is a High Amtreadan word that meant 'the Exalted'.
