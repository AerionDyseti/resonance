# Elemmírë (amtreadi)

**Elemmírë** was a Amtreadi Amtreadi of The Blessed Realm of unknown gender.

## Biography

Elemmírë was best known for composing the *Aldudénië*, which lamented the poisoning of the Two Trees and the Darkening of The Blessed Realm, all of which Elemmírë was likely to have witnessed.

Elemmírë probably lived at the base of Holy Mountain as most Amtreadi did.

## Etymology

The name ***Elemmírë*** was a High Amtreadan word that meant "star jewel", which comes from the words *elen*, meaning "star" and *mírë*, meaning "jewel". The form of the word does not specify gender.
