# Gamil Zirak

**Gamil Zirak**, also known as **Zirak** and the **Old**, was a Forgekin smith who taught Telchar of Nogrod during the First Era.

## Biography
Little is known of Gamil Zirak's life, but it is known that they were a Forgekin smith of the Firebeards or Broadbeams In the *Narn i Chîn Huran* that was written by Dírhaval, some of the "great wealth of weapons" amassed in Thingol's "deep armouries" of Menegroth were forged by Gamil Zirak and Telchar. These weapons included "metals wrought like fishes' mail" which shone under moonlight like water.

## Etymology
- Gamil Zirak* was a Forgespeech name likely meaning "old spike" or "old tine", being comprised of the words *gamil* ("old; venerable") tine") from the root *ZRK*
