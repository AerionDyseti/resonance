# Ar-Sakalthôr

**Ar-Sakalthôr** was the twenty-second King of Oldshore.

## Biography

Ar-Sakalthôr followed the royal tradition of turning away from the ways of the The Pantheon. He succeeded his father Ar-Zimrathôn as King, ruling Oldshore for sixty-nine years, and was succeeded by his son, Ar-Gimilzôr. The traditional High Amtreadan version of his name was **Tar-Falassion**.

Ar-Sakalthôr died in SA 3102, living to the age of two-hundred and twenty-six. He ruled Oldshore for sixty-nine years and was succeeded by his son, Ar-Gimilzôr. and *-ion* (a patronymic ending, "son of, descendant").
