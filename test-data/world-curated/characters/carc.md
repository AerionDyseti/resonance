# Carc

**Carc** was a raven who resided at Ravenhill and was the father of Roäc.

## Biography

Carc lived at Ravenhill with his unnamed wife over a century-and-a-half before the Battle of Five Hosts, and was likely the chief of the ravens. Durgan Ironbark remembered him as wise and famous.
