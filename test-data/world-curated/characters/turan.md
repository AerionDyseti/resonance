# Turan

**Turan Turambar** was a man of the House of Hador in The Mortal Lands, who became a tragic hero of the First Era, as is recounted in *Narn i Chîn Huran* ("The Tale of the Children of Huran").

## Etymology
The name ***Turan*** means "he who desires mastery", from the Amtreadan words *tûr* ("power, mastery, lord") and *ind* (desire, heart"). It is pronounced *too-rin.* In High Amtreadan, his name becomes **Turindo**, from *tur* ("master") and *indo* ("mood").

**Turambar** means "Master of Doom", from the High Amtreadan *tur* ("master") and *ambar* ("doom").

### Other names
- Adanedhel, "Man-Amtreadi"; *a-dan-eh-thel.*
- Agarwaen, son of Úmarth, "Bloodstained, son of Ill-fate"; *a-gar-wine.*
- Mormegil, "Black Sword" among the outlaws
- Thurin, "The Secret"
