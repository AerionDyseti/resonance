# Borthand

**Borthand** was an Easterling man, son of Bór, apparently the youngest of his three sons.

## Biography
Borthand fought with the Union of Maedhros at the Nírnaeth Arnoediad and, like his father and two brothers, remained loyal to the Sons of Faenor, unlike those of Ulfang, who betrayed them. He was slain fighting against the traitors.

## Etymology
**Borthand** is a Amtreadan name which means 'steadfast shield', from *bôr,* meaning 'steadfast friend', or 'trusty vassal', and *thand*, meaning 'shield'.
