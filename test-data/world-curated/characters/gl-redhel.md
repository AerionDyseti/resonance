# Glóredhel

**Glóredhel** was an Edain woman of the House of Hador.

## Biography

Glóredhel was the daughter of Hador and Gildis, and was the eldest sister of Galdor and Gundor. She married Vorniel, Chieftain of the Haladin, and soon gave birth to Handir. In FA 472, Vorniel was killed in the Nírnaeth Arnoediad, causing Glóredhel to die of grief shortly afterwards.

## Etymology

- Glóredhel* means "Golden amtreadi" in Amtreadan. The name comes from the words *Laure*, meaning "golden", and *Edhel*, meaning "amtreadi".
