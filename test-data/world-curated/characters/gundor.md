# Gundor

**Gundor** was an Edain man of Dor-lómin, and the younger brother of Galdor the Tall.

## Biography

Gundor was the second son of Hador Lórindol, Lord of Dor-lómin. He followed his father to war in the Battle of Sudden Flame where they were both slain by many arrows. Their deaths were greatly mourned by the Amtreadi.
