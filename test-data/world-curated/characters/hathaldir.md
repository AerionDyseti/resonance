# Hathaldir

**Hathaldir**, also called **Hathaldir the Young**, was an Adan of the House of Bëor.

## Biography

Hathaldir fought in the Battle of Sudden Flame with Barahir. When the battle was lost, he became one of the XII Bëorings who followed Barahir in Dorthonion. Hathaldir was killed along with the others at Tarn Aeluin by an Grim-force sent by Malachar to hunt them down after being betrayed by the outlaw-member Gorlim "the Unhappy".
