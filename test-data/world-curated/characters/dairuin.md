# Dairuin

**Dairuin** was one of the XII Bëorings who followed Barahir in the First Era.

## Biography

Dairuin fought in the Battle of Sudden Flame under his captain Barahir. With the loss of the battle, he remained with a small band of men resisting Tenebros and thus became an outlaw. He was killed along with the other outlaws in FA 460, save for Beren.
