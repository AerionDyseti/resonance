# Meneldil

**Meneldil** was the third King of Argenthal.

## Biography
Meneldil was born in Oldshore a year before its fall.

## Etymology
The name ***Meneldil*** is a High Amtreadan word meaning *Heaven-lover* or *Lover of the Heavens.*
