# Borlach

**Borlach** was an Easterling of the First Era, one of three sons of Bór.

## Biography
Borlach was an Easterling Man from Lothlann, alive during the late First Era. He was the son of Bór and fought with the Union of Maedhros at the Nírnaeth Arnoediad. Like his father and his two brothers Borlad and Borthand, Borlach remained loyal to the Sons of Faenor, unlike the Easterlings who followed Ulfang, who betrayed them. He was slain in the battle, while fighting against the traitors.

## Etymology
The name **Borlach** means 'trusty fire', from *bôr*, meaning 'steadfast friend', or 'trusty vassal', and *lach*, meaning 'fire', or 'leaping fire'.
