## Background

High amtreadi Wizard/Vessel and one of only three remaining Dragonmarked, bearing Aranada of Temnis—the golden dragon of arcane knowledge and magical power. Mentored by Cais, Speaker of the Amtreadi. Through mystical connection with Arkanis Maegus ex Tiameti (The Librarian), her vast intellect expanded exponentially. When her lover Niko'asai died, she led complex political negotiations in Osiria to secure a resurrection ritual, which ultimately succeeded in bringing him back.

[Role/Occupation: Wizard/Vessel, Dragonmarked Bearer]

## Personality

Methodical. Intellectually driven. Strategic thinker who transforms problems into elegantly documented theoretical frameworks. Explorer of the unknown.

## Appearance

High amtreadi bearing Aranada of Temnis—a golden dragonmark visible on her skin.

### Notable Characteristics

- One of only three remaining Dragonmarked
- Mystical connection to The Librarian enhanced her intellect
- Transforms complex problems into theoretical frameworks
- Diplomatic skill in high-stakes negotiations

## Relationships

Lover of Niko'asai, whom she successfully resurrected after his death. Mentored by Cais, Speaker of the Amtreadi. Connected to Arkanis Maegus ex Tiameti (The Librarian) through mystical bond.

## Goals and Aspirations

- Explore and catalog unknown magical phenomena
- Carry the burden and duty of Aranada of Temnis
- Protect those she loves
