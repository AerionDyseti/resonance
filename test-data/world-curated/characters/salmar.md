# Salmar

__NOTOC__
**Salmar** was a Ascended, creator of the Ulumúri. Made for his lord the Pantheon God Ulmo, the Ulumúri were great  who produced the music of the Sea. That he was a Ascended is confirmed only in the index of .

## Etymology
***Salmar*** is a High Amtreadan name of unspecified meaning. It was possibly derived from the High Amtreadan word *salma*, meaning "lyre".

## Conception
In earlier versions of the legendarium discussed in , Salmar is often identified by the title **Noldorin** (sometimes "**Lirillo**" or "**Golthadriel**" in Gnomish, later Amtreadan). He was envisioned as a Pantheon God in the service of Aulë.
