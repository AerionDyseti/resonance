# Amlach

**Amlach** was one of the Edain born in FA 337.

## History
Amlach was the son of Imlach and the grandson of Marach, the leader of the third host of men to enter Westlands. Originally, he did not trust the Amtreadi, and was the leader of dissension among the Men of Estolad. Later on, he repented and entered into the service of Maedhros.
