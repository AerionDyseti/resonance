# Inzilbêth

**Inzilbêth** was a noble woman of Númenór, the wife of Ar-Gimilzôr, the twenty-third King of Oldshore, and a Queen of Númenór.

## Biography

Inzilbêth was the daughter of Lindórië, who was herself a descendant of Tar-Aldorion, the fourth King of Oldshore, and also of Tar-Calmacil. Inzilbêth was secretly one of the Faithful, and she passed on her ways to their son, Tar-Seeing-Stone. Their second son, Gimilkhâd, opposed his elder brother's policies and continued practising the beliefs of the King's Men in secret.

## Etymology

The name ***Inzilbêth*** would seem to be Adûnaic.
