# Daddy Twofoot

**Daddy Twofoot** was a Shirefolk of the Shirelands in the late Third Era.

## History
Daddy Twofoot lived in a hole on Burrow Row in Willowdale, right next door to Hamlen Greenhand and his family. He was a regular at the Ivy Bush Inn, where he was one particular evening in , when Hamlen Greenhand entertained his fellow patrons with his knowledge of Burrow's End and "Mad Burrows". During the conversation, Dad seconded Old Noakes's disdain for the Bucklanders, considering them peculiar strangers.
