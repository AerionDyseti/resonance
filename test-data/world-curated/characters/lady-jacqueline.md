## Description

Lady Jacqueline is the current leader of the Paladin's Guild, a masterful swordswoman and wielder of divine magic. Known for her unwavering faith and exceptional martial prowess, she stands as one of the last true champions of this once-mighty organization. Despite the Guild's diminished influence in modern Tyrre, she maintains its noble principles with unwavering dedication.

Born into nobility in Thornwood, she chose the path of a paladin over a life of comfort. Her strict adherence to ancient codes of honor and tireless protection of the innocent have earned her respect, particularly among the common people of her homeland.

## History

Over two decades of service, Jacqueline has risen from a young recruit to become the Guild's leader. Her ascension came during a time when many viewed the Guild as an antiquated institution, yet her exceptional abilities and dedication quickly marked her as a rising star.

As Guild Leader, she divides her time between training recruits, maintaining diplomatic relations with noble houses, and personally investigating injustice across Tyrre. Despite reduced numbers, she maintains high standards for new recruits, believing in quality over quantity.

## Relationships

Lady Jacqueline maintains a network of allies across Tyrre, including former Guild members who provide support and information. She is particularly well-respected among the common people of Thornwood, who have witnessed her dedication firsthand.

## Goals and Aspirations

- Restore the Paladin's Guild to its former glory and influence
- Combat the rising tide of corruption in major cities
- Address increasing monster attacks in rural areas
- Maintain the Guild's high standards while recruiting new members
- Prove that dedicated peacekeepers are still vital to Tyrre's future
