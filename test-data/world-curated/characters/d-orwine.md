# Déorwine

**Déorwine** was the Chief of the King's Riders during the War of the Binding.

## History

Déorwine replaced Hagan in the post when the latter was killed at Thornwall Keep. He and six of the men under his command (Harding, Guthláf, Herefara, Herubrand, Horn and Fastred) were slain at the Battle of Galim Vir Fields.

## Etymology

The name _Déorwine_ probably means "brave-friend" in Old English, consisting of _dēor_ ("brave, bold (as a wild beast)" + _wine_ ("friend").
