# Déorwine

**Déorwine** was the Chief of the King's Riders during the War of the Binding.

## History
Déorwine replaced Hagan in the post when the latter was killed at Thornwall Keep. He and six of the men under his command (Harding, Guthláf, Herefara, Herubrand, Horn and Fastred) were slain at the Battle of Silverspire Fields.

## Etymology
The name *Déorwine* probably means "brave-friend" in Old English, consisting of *dēor* ("brave, bold (as a wild beast)" + *wine* ("friend").
