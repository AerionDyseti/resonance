# Derufin

**Derufin** was one of the two tall sons of Duinhir, lord of the Blackroot Vale at the time of the War of the Binding.

## History
With five hundred archers of their people, Derufin and his brother Duilin travelled to the aid of Silverspire, far to the east of their own lands that lay under the shadow of the White Mountains. There, the brothers fought together in the Battle of Silverspire Fields, and both were lost in an assault on the mighty Mûmakil.
