## Background

Forgekin of the Adamantine Oath clan, renowned for honor in business and exceptional metalwork. Oversees the fine jewelry trade in Four Corners and serves as a pillar of mercantile etiquette, negotiating firmly but fairly. Protects clan reputation and insists on transparent weights, measures, and provenance in every deal.

## Personality

Aristocratic bearing. Exacting standards. Courteous but unbending when it comes to quality or contracts. Values reputation over short‑term profit.

## Appearance

Iron‑gray beard braided with understated platinum clasps. Tailored trader’s coat with polished metal accents and a jeweler’s loupe on a chain.

### Notable Characteristics

- Calibrates his own scales before any negotiation
- Keeps a pocket ledger of assay results
- Smells faintly of oilstone and hot metal

## Relationships

Represents the Adamantine Oath in guild councils. Maintains supplier networks among artisan clans and select human ateliers. Courteous rivalry with other jewel guilds in Four Corners.

## Goals and Aspirations

- Elevate Four Corners as the continent’s premier jewelry exchange
- Stamp out forgery and adulterated alloys
- Mentor a new generation of oath‑bound appraisers
