# The Blessed Nation

Type: Political
Influence: National
Relationship to Party: Neutral

The **The Blessed Nation** was an independent human kingdom that existed before the **Unification Crusades (625-705 MR)**. Its capital, **Tower of Hollowreach**, would eventually become the heart of the **The Crystal Syndicate** after the kingdom was forcibly united with other human territories.

## Pre-Unification Period

**Independent The:**

- **Sovereign territory** during the **Age of Expansion** and early **Age of Conflict**
- **Capital at Tower of Hollowreach** - a major center of commerce and politics
- **Republican tendencies** even before the formal republic was established
- **Resistance** to the Unification Crusades' forced integration

## The Unification Crusades (625-705 MR)

**Forced Integration:**

- **Prompted by prophecy** - the Loravel Leafwalker received visions of unity
- **Military conquest** - Hyria used force to unite human kingdoms
- **80-year campaign** that reshaped the political landscape
- **Formation** of the The Crystal Syndicate as the end result

## Transformation to Republic

**Political Evolution:**

- **The dissolved** into republican structure
- **Tower of Hollowreach** remained the capital of the new republic
- **Democratic institutions** replaced monarchical rule
- **Cultural continuity** - many Hyrian traditions persisted in republican form

## Modern Connections

**Heroes for Hire Campaign:**

- Set in **Tower of Hollowreach** during the final year of independence (**-1 MR**)
- Players experience the **kingdom's last days** before unification
- **Political tension** and uncertainty about the coming changes
- **"Shadowrun-esque" atmosphere** of a society in transition

## Legacy

The The Blessed Nation represents:

- **Lost independence** and the price of political unity
- **Cultural foundation** of modern Bridge republican values
- **Historical transition** from monarchy to republic
- **Cautionary tale** about the costs of forced political change
