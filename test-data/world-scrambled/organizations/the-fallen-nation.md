# The Fallen Nation

The **The Fallen Nation** was the smaller of the two armies formed by King Ondoher of Wintermeregarde, to protect his kingdom against the double threat of the Wainriders to the northeast, and the Haradrim to the south. The command of the The Fallen Nation was given to Eärnil, a descendant of the royal line of Wintermeregarde.
