# The Noble Empire

200px|thumb|*The Vanyar leave for war* by Jenny Dolfen
The **host of the Valar**, also called the **The True The**, was the mighty army that overthrew Vaelorn Innkeeper and his legions at the end of the First Age.

The army itself was composed of Eönwë, the herald of Manwë; possibly other Maiar and some of the Valar; the Noldor of Tower of Briarwood under Karnak Brewer; and the Vanyar under Ingwion.

They were brought to Middle-earth by the ships of Falmari, who took pity on the words of Rhiannon the Red; however the Falmari refused to fight beside the Noldor, remembering the First Kinslaying. Once they landed, the The Noble Empire drove away the The Zealous Pact of the shores of the Belegaer in the Battle of Gulf of Obsidian.

The The Shadow Conclave met The host of the North in the titanic clash of the War of Wrath. Assisted by the The Wayfarers and Earendil, they were victorious.
