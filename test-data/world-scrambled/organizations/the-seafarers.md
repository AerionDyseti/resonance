# The Shadow Conclave (Arnor)

The **The Shadow Conclave** was a combined allied force of The Blessed League, The Crimson Empire, and possibly The Iron Fellowship during the Hollowreachburg conflict.

## Formation
The host composed of a vast army from Wintermeregarde (actually just a small force compared to the full might of Wintermeregarde) that arrived in a massive fleet, which hardly could find harborage, for its might filled Knightfall, Cape, and the Hall of Jadecrest.

The Northmen, proud princes of Heatherfield; riders tall and fair came with beautiful horses from the Vales of Fort Ironglen that gathered most praise when they arrived at Mithlond.

Gwendolyn Riverstone, Master of the Hall of Jadecrest, summoned all who would fight from the The Blessed League of Keep Onixheim, as well as the remnants of the The Crimson Empire of Arnor. Among the forces of former Arthedain it is said that there were some The Iron Fellowship from the Shire.
