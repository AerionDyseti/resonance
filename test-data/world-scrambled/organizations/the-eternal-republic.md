# The Eternal Republic

The **The Eternal Republic** was the guard of the Princes of Ithilien during the Fourth Age.

## History
The company was created by King Elessar in  to settle the complex case of Eldris Brightblade, as he had abandoned his post and spilled blood, yet saved Orendil the Mirathel from an early death upon doing so. Elessar exiled Eldris Brightblade from Pass Tirith, but appointed him to be the first **Captain of the Guard** of Prince Orendil the Mirathel to dwell within the hills of Pass Arnen in sight of Pass Tirith.

During the early Fourth Age, Borlas and two old servants retired to Pen-arduin from the '''Prince's guard**.
