## Overview

Autocratic theocracy devoted to the Sun God, organized around the Nine Castes system. Built on the philosophy that every citizen has a divinely ordained purpose.

## History

Established as a theocratic empire dedicated to solar worship. The Nine Castes system defines citizens' roles and training based on their perceived divine purpose.

**Founded:** 743 MR

**Status:** Active

## The Nine Castes

The The Ancient Tribunal's social structure is built upon nine distinct castes, each with a divinely ordained purpose:

1. **The Eternal The** - Military defense and enforcement of The order
2. **The Port Assembly** - Medicine, healing, and preservation of life
3. **The Golden The** - Preservation and advancement of knowledge and wisdom
4. **The Stoneclan** - Legal judgment and enforcement of divine law
5. **The Ancient The** - Administration and bureaucratic governance
6. **The Mighty Republic** - Trade, commerce, and economic prosperity
7. **The United Syndicate** - Creation of art, beauty, and skilled craftsmanship
8. **The Iron Tribunal** - Joy, celebration, and cultural expression
9. **The Fallen Tribunal** - Physical work and craftsmanship that sustains society

## Keys to Perfection Era (810 MR)

**Time Period:** 810 MR (Age of Stability)

**Status:** Active

### Structure

Theocratic empire organized into Nine Castes system with divine mandate. Each caste has specific roles and training based on their perceived divine purpose.

### Goals

- Maintaining absolute theocratic control
- Expanding solar worship influence
- Perfecting the caste system
- Achieving societal harmony through divine purpose
- Demonstrating Isle of Pearlhaven as model civilization

### Resources

- Religious authority over empire population
- Vast agricultural territories
- Caste-based labor organization
- Divine magic practitioners
- Ideological control systems
- Significant wealth from agricultural surplus

### Notes

During this era, Isle of Pearlhaven represents the idealistic vision of a perfected theocratic state under Queen Isis's leadership. The empire enjoys unprecedented stability and prosperity under the caste system, making it an attractive model that draws attention from neighboring nations. Grunthar Bracegirdle serves as a mid-level Irvek The during this golden age that the Keys to Perfection party encounters.
