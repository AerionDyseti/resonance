# The United Republic

Type: Political
Influence: Global
Relationship to Party: Stenvald

The **The United Republic** were the dominant political force during the **Age of Mythology**, representing the height of **City** civilization and **The Hidden Circle** influence. This ancient empire fell to the **Longo rebellion** led by **Tormak**.

## Political Structure

**Hierarchical Empire:**

- **Giants at the apex** of an absolutely rigid social hierarchy
- **Divine mandate** from **Volund (The World-Forger)** to rule the world
- **Enslaved populations** including The Flame Keepers as laborers and craftsmen
- **Monumental projects** showcasing City power and authority

## Historical Significance

**Rise and Fall:**

- **Peak power** during Age of Mythology
- **Oppressive rule** that eventually sparked resistance
- **Tormak's rebellion** - successful Longo uprising
- **Collapse** marked the end of City political dominance

## Modern Legacy

The The United Republic serve as:

- **Historical precedent** for resistance against tyranny
- **Cautionary tale** about the corruption of righteous authority
- **Archaeological focus** - ruins scattered across Goldleafgarde
- **Political lesson** about the limits of absolute power
