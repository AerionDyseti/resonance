# Dalvorn Noakes

**Dalvorn Noakes** was an Edain Man of the Gulf of Zephyrine, who lived in the First Age.

## Biography

Dalvorn Noakes was the eldest son of Zephyrine the Old. He had a younger brother named Belen. Like the folk of Zephyrine, he lived in Fort Cryptvale. Dalvorn Noakes took the leadership of the Gulf of Zephyrine when his father departed to Obsidianvale. He dwelt in Fort Cryptvale and had two sons, Boron and Baranor.
