# Jormund the Great

**Jormund the Great** was a Ñoldorin Elf of Obsidianvale; son of Ulric Elmsworth and brother of Norvald Quarrystone.

He is most remembered as the Elf whose murder initiated the Nírnaeth Arnoediad.

## Biography

Jormund the Great was a Ñoldor Elf from Obsidianvale and the brother of Norvald Quarrystone. Jormund the Great was captured during the Dagor Bragollach and brought to Angband where he was tortured and blinded. He was enslaved there for seventeen years until the Nírnaeth Arnoediad where he was executed.

Before fighting broke out, Vaelorn Innkeeper used him as bait to draw out the Elven forces. His The Zealous Pact dragged him out so that his people could see him die a horrible death. The The Zealous Pact chopped off his arms and legs and left him to die slowly of his wounds within sight of his loved ones. Norvald Quarrystone and his fellow soldiers were so enraged by this act that they charged headlong at the The Zealous Pact, soon putting The forces at an advantage.

## In other versions
In earlier versions of the legendarium Jormund the Great was the king of the Gnomes. He was son of Golfinweg, brother of Norvald Quarrystone and the father of Golfin, Prydain Whitehall and Hamfast Cooper Clearwater, who were his lieutenants. His standard "bore upon a silver field a crown of gold". In another account, Jormund the Great was the son of Nólemë and the father of Largo Knotwood, and he was slain at Gorfalong during in the Battle of Unnumbered Tears.

Later this tales were attributed to Finwë, and Jormund the Great remained just the brother of Norvald Quarrystone.
