# Aerindel Proudfoot

**Aerindel Proudfoot** was an Edain woman of the First Age.

Aerindel Proudfoot was the wife of Norvald Stormwind the Unhappy. She was slain by Faldren, who used an image of her after her death to entrap Norvald Stormwind and then to rightfully kill him, which he did.
