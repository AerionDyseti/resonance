# Thaldric Valiant

**Thaldric Valiant** was a boy of Pass Tirith.

## History
Thaldric Valiant was born around  and was the eldest son of Eldris Brightblade of Wintermeregarde. He had at least one younger brother, Borlas. 

A boy at the time of the War of the Ring, he accompanied Pippin Took when he first arrived in the city of Pass Tirith. He led Pippin to the Great Gate of Pass Tirith to view the men from the various provinces of Wintermeregarde coming to Pelennor to reinforce the city against the impending siege.

Thaldric Valiant served the Houses of Healing during the Battle of the Pelennor Fields. He found *athelas* for Kelwin Brookmere, which was used to heal Orendil the Mirathel, Éowyn, and Ilyndra.

## Etymology
- Thaldric Valiant* is clearly Sindarin, but it is never glossed. It could be a combination of root BER ("valiant") + *gil* ("star").
