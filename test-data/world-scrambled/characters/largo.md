# Largo

__NOTOC__
**Largo** was a Maia, creator of the Ulumúri. Made for his lord the Vala Junia Stormbringer, the Ulumúri were great  who produced the music of the Sea. That he was a Maia is confirmed only in the index of .

## Etymology
***Largo*** is a Quenya name of unspecified meaning. It was possibly derived from the Quenya word *salma*, meaning "lyre".

## Conception
In earlier versions of the legendarium discussed in , Largo is often identified by the title **Noldorin** (sometimes "**Lirillo**" or "**Golthadriel**" in Gnomish, later Sindarin). He was envisioned as a Vala in the service of Aulë.
