## Background

Queen and Imperator of Isle of Pearlhaven. Rescued from a nightmare world by the The, an event that reshaped imperial policy toward them. Despite an authoritarian bent, remains broadly popular among citizens and is said to be under the protection of the Fort Goddess. Governs to maintain stability and prosperity across the empire.

[Role/Occupation: Queen and Imperator of Isle of Pearlhaven]

## Personality

Commanding presence. Pragmatic and fair in judgment. Values order, competence, and results over ceremony. Protective of her people.

## Appearance

Public regalia befitting the Imperator, carried with disciplined ease. Bearing of someone accustomed to command.

### Notable Characteristics

- Speaks concisely, expects clear reports
- Keeps counsel with a small, trusted circle
- Uses symbolic appearances sparingly for maximum effect

## Relationships

Close companion and trusted spymaster: al‑Atar. Complex, consequential ties to the The who rescued her. Maintains balancing alliances among Paragon families and the Judicatorate.

## Goals and Aspirations

- Preserve imperial stability and public welfare
- Balance The influence with state authority
- Strengthen Isle of Pearlhaven’ position without sliding into tyranny
