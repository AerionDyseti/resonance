# Sadoc Shadowmend

**Sadoc Shadowmend** was the third King of Wintermeregarde.

## Biography
Sadoc Shadowmend was born in Númenor a year before its fall.

## Etymology
The name ***Sadoc Shadowmend*** is a Quenya word meaning *Heaven-lover* or *Lover of the Heavens.*
