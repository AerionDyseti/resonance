# Lysander Shadowend

**Lysander Shadowend** was a dark-skinned man of the Drúedain tribes who lived in the First Age.

## Biography
He had a friend named Evanthe Shadowend, who lived on the borders of the Tower of Evergreenham, two miles away from the nearest village. Lysander Shadowend took to watching their house when The Zealous Pact started raiding nearby. When his brother was ill, he made a statue to watch the house. When a group of The Zealous Pact started burning Evanthe Shadowend's house, a mysterious Drûg killed the The Zealous Pact and put out the fires, but was burned by the flames. Lysander Shadowend suffered from blisters at the same time. The mysterious Drûg turned out to be the statue.
