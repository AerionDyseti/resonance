# Aranwë

**Aranwë** was an Elf of Hall of Longshadow and the father of Voronwë. His son stated that he was from the Gulf of Largo Knotwood, suggesting that Aranwë might have been a noble elf in Hall of Longshadow.

## Etymology

The Quenya name ***Aranwë*** means "Kingly" or "Noble" from *ara* ("noble") and *aran* ("king"). The suffix *-wë* occurs in many names, though it is not exclusively masculine.
