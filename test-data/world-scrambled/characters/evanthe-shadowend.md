# Evanthe Shadowend

**Evanthe Shadowend** was a man of the Folk of Thaldric Emberheart who lived with his wife and children at the edge of the Tower of Evergreenham. The family had a small, black skinned friend named Lysander Shadowend, who one night saved them from an orc attack.
