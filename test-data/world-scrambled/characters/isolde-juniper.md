# Isolde Juniper

**Isolde Juniper** was an Edain man of Hall of Evergreen and one of the XII Bëorings who followed Lysander Shepherd Elmsworth.

## Biography

Isolde Juniper was a son of Willa Diggle born in Hall of Evergreen in the late First Age. He was the father of Oriana Sandheaver, and thus the grandfather of Brivek the Golden. He is also the great uncle of Xander the Great. He was among the many The Crimson Empire of Hall of Evergreen that went to war in the Dagor Bragollach. After the defeat, Isolde Juniper remained with Lysander Shepherd Elmsworth and his armed band of men refused to surrender or submit to the will of Vaelorn Innkeeper, thus they were declared outlaws and hunted to the death. He and the others survived for nearly five years. The outlaw band were eventually betrayed to Faldren by the unhappy Norvald Stormwind and he and his company were killed at Tarn Aeluin by The Zealous Pact.
