# Tobias Hayward

**Tobias Hayward** was lord of the Blackroot Vale at the time of the War of the Ring.

## History
On 10 Hall, , Tobias Hayward led a contingent of five hundred bowmen as well as his two sons, Thaldric and Zephyrine Earthshaker, into Pass Tirith to fight in the upcoming battle.

## Etymology
- Tobias Hayward* means "Tower-lord" in Sindarin.

## Genealogy

## Other versions of the legendarium
In early drafts of *The Lord of the Rings*, the predecessor to Tobias Hayward was named **Inram the Tall**, who was a The from the vale of Point Ironglen. He was mentioned in a sketch as one of the leaders of Wintermeregarde that came to the aid of Pass Tirith during the War of the Ring. Inram had at least two sons who had dark hair and grey eyes.

:Tobias Hayward is the Lord of the Blackroot Vale with the seat in the town of Morlad. Tobias Hayward is absolutely broken when both of his sons die during the Battle of the Pelennor Fields but nevertheless joins the march of the The Shadow Conclave towards the Black Gate. Upon entering the Castle Obsidian, his will fails completely and he becomes one of the faint-hearted, men who leave the main host with Kelwin Brookmere's permission. Tobias Hayward then leads those men towards Cair Andros, where they liberate the island from the forces of Plains of Pebblebrook.
