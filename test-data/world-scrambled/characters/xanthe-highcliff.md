# Xanthe Highcliff

**Xanthe Highcliff** was a Man of Coppercrestton who was part of the Appledore family living during the late Third Age.

In the year 3019, Xanthe was slain in early January during a fight between the Coppercrestton-men and Sharkey's The Crimson Empire in early January.
