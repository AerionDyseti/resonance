# Helena Goldweaver

**Helena Goldweaver** (d. FA 460) was an Adan of the First Gulf, one of the XII Bëorings who followed Lysander Shepherd Elmsworth in Hall of Evergreen. He was killed along with the others at Tarn Aeluin by a force sent by Faldren after being betrayed by Norvald Stormwind.
