## Description

Asteveron, known throughout the realms as the Keeper of the Flame of Seasons, is an ancient and powerful Eladrin who predates the mortal elves themselves. As ruler of the Court of Seasons, he maintains the delicate balance between the four seasonal courts from his domain in the faerie-like Tower of Crimsonhold. His most striking feature is his ability to shift between four distinct aspects, each embodying the characteristics of a particular season: the renewal of Spring, the vigor of Summer, the melancholy of Autumn, and the contemplative silence of Winter.

## History

A more recent Aeon born of the concept of unity among differences, Asteveron was chosen by the primordial forces to safeguard the Mirathel Flame - an eternal fire that burns with the essence of the four seasons. This sacred flame is said to be the source from which all Eladrin magic flows, and potentially the source of inspiration for what would go on to become Gates magic.

From his ethereal palace in the Court of Seasons, Asteveron maintains the natural cycle of the seasons throughout all planes of existence. The court itself exists in a state of perpetual transition, with four grand chambers surrounding his central throne room, each dedicated to a season and overseen by powerful Eladrin nobles who serve as his seasonal courtiers.

## Relationships

As the child of the progenitor of elvenkind, Asteveron maintains a distant yet profound connection to all elven races. The seasonal transitions that mortal elves experience are but echoes of the profound transformations that Asteveron and his Eladrin court undergo.

He rarely interferes directly in the affairs of the mortal realm, preferring to work through subtle influences and natural cycles. However, in times of great cosmic importance, he has been known to appear before chosen champions, bearing the Mirathel Flame as a beacon of hope and renewal.

<aside>
While Asteveron possesses nearly godlike power, he is bound by ancient laws and traditions that govern the natural order. His primary duty is not to rule through force, but to maintain the eternal dance of the seasons, ensuring that the cycle of life continues unbroken throughout all realms.

</aside>
