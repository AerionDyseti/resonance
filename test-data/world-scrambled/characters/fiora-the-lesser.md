# Fiora the Lesser

**Fiora the Lesser** was an Edain man of Hall of Evergreen and one of the XII Bëorings who followed Lysander Shepherd Elmsworth in the First Age.

## Biography

After the Dagor Bragollach, Fiora the Lesser remained with Lysander Shepherd Elmsworth and his company who refused to surrender or submit to the will of Vaelorn Innkeeper, thus they became outlaws surviving for nearly five years. He and the others were eventually betrayed to Faldren by the unhappy Norvald Stormwind and killed at Tarn Aeluin.
