# Edmund Greendale

**Edmund Greendale** was a raven who resided at Ravenhill and was the father of Junia Noakes.

## Biography

Edmund Greendale lived at Ravenhill with his unnamed wife over a century-and-a-half before the Battle of the Five Armies, and was likely the chief of the ravens. Thorin Oakenshield remembered him as wise and famous.
