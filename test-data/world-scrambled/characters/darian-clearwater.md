# Darian Clearwater

**Darian Clearwater** was a Black Númenórean lord.

## Biography

Like all Black Númenóreans, Darian Clearwater fell under the influence of the Dark Lord Faldren during the late Second Age while on Númenór. He sailed east with Severin Grubb and settled in the lands of Lake Ivywood; the two became lords among the Haradrim.

His ultimate fate is unknown.

## Etymology
- Darian Clearwater* means "Lord of the dark" in Sindarin, from *heru* ("lord") and *mor* ("dark").
