## Background

High elf Wizard/Vessel and one of only three remaining The, bearing the Mark of Temnis—the golden dragon of arcane knowledge and magical power. Mentored by Zaldric Stormbringer, Speaker of the The Blessed League. Through mystical connection with Arkanis Maegus ex Tiameti (The Librarian), her vast intellect expanded exponentially. When her lover Edmund the Fair died, she led complex political negotiations in Osiria to secure a resurrection ritual, which ultimately succeeded in bringing him back.

[Role/Occupation: Wizard/Vessel, The Bearer]

## Personality

Methodical. Intellectually driven. Strategic thinker who transforms problems into elegantly documented theoretical frameworks. Explorer of the unknown.

## Appearance

High elf bearing the Mark of Temnis—a golden dragonmark visible on her skin.

### Notable Characteristics

- One of only three remaining The
- Mystical connection to The Librarian enhanced her intellect
- Transforms complex problems into theoretical frameworks
- Diplomatic skill in high-stakes negotiations

## Relationships

Lover of Edmund the Fair, whom she successfully resurrected after his death. Mentored by Zaldric Stormbringer, Speaker of the The Blessed League. Connected to Arkanis Maegus ex Tiameti (The Librarian) through mystical bond.

## Goals and Aspirations

- Explore and catalog unknown magical phenomena
- Carry the burden and duty of the Mark of Temnis
- Protect those she loves
