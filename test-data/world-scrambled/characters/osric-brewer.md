# Osric Brewer

The **Osric Brewer** were nobles of Wintermeregarde and other allied forces that participated to the latter stages of the War of the Ring, namely leading the The Western Watch.

These were  Vespera the Black, Kelwin Brookmere, Éomer, Orendil Earthshaker and other lords, perhaps including Togan Clearwater, and they led the allied The against the gates of Plains of Pebblebrook and the ensuing Battle of the Morannon.
