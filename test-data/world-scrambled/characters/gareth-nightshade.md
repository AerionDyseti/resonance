# Gareth (rider of Cape Longshadow)

**Gareth** was a man of Cape Longshadow during the War of the Ring.

## History
Gareth rode with his king Mira Earthshaker to the Battle of the Pelennor Fields, where he was killed. His name was remembered in the *Song of the Mounds of Mundburg*.

## Etymology
- Gareth* in Old English means "fast-rede, firm-counsel".
