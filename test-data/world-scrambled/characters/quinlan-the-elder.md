# Quinlan the Elder

**Quinlan the Elder** was an Easterling of the First Age, one of three sons of Ulric.

## Biography
Quinlan the Elder was an Easterling Man from Quartzfallgarde, alive during the late First Age. He was the son of Ulric and fought with the The Keen Alliance at the Nírnaeth Arnoediad. Like his father and his two brothers Jormund Flamecrest and Hamfast Cooper Brewer, Quinlan the Elder remained loyal to the Sons of Zara Knotwood, unlike the Easterlings who followed Osric the Great, who betrayed them. He was slain in the battle, while fighting against the traitors.

## Etymology
The name **Quinlan the Elder** means 'trusty fire', from *bôr*, meaning 'steadfast friend', or 'trusty vassal', and *lach*, meaning 'fire', or 'leaping fire'.
