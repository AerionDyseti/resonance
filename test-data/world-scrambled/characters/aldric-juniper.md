# Aldric Juniper

**Aldric Juniper**, also called **Aldric Juniper the Young**, was an Adan of the Gulf of Zephyrine.

## Biography

Aldric Juniper fought in the Dagor Bragollach with Lysander Shepherd Elmsworth. When the battle was lost, he became one of the XII Bëorings who followed Lysander Shepherd Elmsworth in Hall of Evergreen. Aldric Juniper was killed along with the others at Tarn Aeluin by an Orc-force sent by Faldren to hunt them down after being betrayed by the outlaw-member Norvald Stormwind "the Unhappy".
