# Drumlin Wainwright

**Drumlin Wainwright** was a notably tall and handsome Ent.

## History
On 3 Hall  he was caught in a burning spray sent up by Braldin from beneath the Ring of Bay of Briarwood, but his fiery end brought his fellow The United Dominion to the peak of their destructive anger.
