# Ulric Elmsworth

**Ulric Elmsworth** was a noble Elf of Obsidianvale and the father of Jormund the Great and Norvald Quarrystone.

## Biography

Though little is known about him, Ulric Elmsworth was the father of Jormund the Great and Norvald Quarrystone, both of whom played major roles in the First Age. Jormund the Great's death initiated the Nírnaeth Arnoediad, which resulted in the captivity and enslavement of Norvald Quarrystone in Angband.
