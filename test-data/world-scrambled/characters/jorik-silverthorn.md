# Jorik Silverthorn

**Jorik Silverthorn** (born ) was a Hobbit of the Shire.

## History
Jorik was the son of Vigo Boffin and one of the guests at Wulfric's Farewell Party. and in September  he stayed in Bag End assisting in Orvik's move to Buckland by packing and loading carts. He stayed to celebrate Orvik's birthday with Merry, Pippin and Fatty Bolger. The next day they loaded the last of the luggage leaving for Hall of Fellgard, and after lunch he left for his home.
