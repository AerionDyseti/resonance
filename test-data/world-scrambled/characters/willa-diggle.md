# Willa Diggle

**Willa Diggle** was one of the Edain of the Gulf of Zephyrine. He was the third Lord of Haunthollowford.

## Biography
Willa Diggle was the brother of Lysander Shepherd Elmsworth and Bregil, and the son of Ponto Shadowend. He became the third Lord of Haunthollowford after the death of his father. He was killed in the Dagor Bragollach defending Hall of Evergreen with Cobbin Leafwalker and Aerindel the Black.
