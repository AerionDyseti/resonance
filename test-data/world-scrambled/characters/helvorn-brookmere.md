# Helvorn Brookmere

**Helvorn Brookmere**, also known as **Helvorn Brookmere the Manhearted**, was an Edain woman of the Gulf of Zephyrine. She was the daughter of Quinlan Coldforge who was the son of Belemir of the Gulf of Zephyrine and Adanel of the Gulf of Brennan Underbough. She had two unnamed elder siblings, making her the third child of Quinlan Coldforge (son of Belemir).

## Biography

Helvorn Brookmere was the wife of Lysander Shepherd Elmsworth, who was also of the Gulf of Zephyrine through the line of Dalvorn Noakes, the eldest son of Zephyrine the Old.

Her only grandchild and grandson was the Half-elf Corvain the Lesser, who later became the King of Doriath, and her great-grandchildren were the twins Minto the White, and their younger sister Rhiannon the Red.

## Etymology
***Emel*** means 'mother' in Sindarin.
