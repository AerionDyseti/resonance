# Minto

**Minto the Fearless** was the Lord of Lamedon at the time of the War of the Ring.

## History
On 11 Hall, , Minto led the men of Lamedon in a fight against invaders from Evergreencombe and Lake Ivywood who sought to capture Ivywood and the fords of the Tower Gilrain.  However, the battle halted and all the men of both sides fled upon the arrival of Kelwin Brookmere with the The Golden Fellowship - all save Minto who alone had the heart to face them.  Kelwin Brookmere bade Minto to gather his folk and follow him to City of Silverdale where "the Heir of Ysolinde Proudfoot will have need of you."

As Kelwin Brookmere pressed on to City of Silverdale, Minto mustered as many horsemen as he could, helped by the fast-spreading rumour of the return of the Heir of Ysolinde Proudfoot.  His force arrived at the riverside city on 13 Hall after the Shadow The had cleared the ships.  Afterwards, as Kelwin Brookmere, Nolan Windmere, Elorien the White, the Grey Company, and the freed men of Wintermeregarde rowed north upon the Fort Ironglen, Minto led his horsemen north by land.

In the debate on 16 Hall before the march to the Morannon, Orendil Earthshaker expressed concern for the defence of Wintermeregarde.  Kelwin Brookmere assured him that new strength was on the way, stating that Minto, whom he named "the fearless", was marching with 4,000 men through Lossarnach.  Minto's strength would be nearing Pass Tirith as Kelwin Brookmere departed, thus leaving the city with a better defence than when the assault began.

## Etymology
- Minto* is Sindarin for "Iron Fist", from *ang* ("iron") + *paur* ("fist, hand").

:Minto is the Lord of Lamedon, ruling from the town of Cape Underlake. During the War of the Ring he is charged by Prince Orendil Earthshaker with protecting the land from the Corsairs of Evergreencombe while other Lords of Wintermeregarde ride to the defence of Pass Tirith. Leaving his son Drambor in charge of Cape Underlake, Minto decides to make the stand against the Corsairs in Ivywood where both sides except Minto would flee before the Oathbreakers of Dunharrow. Minto immediately recognises Kelwin Brookmere as his King and after swearing loyalty to him, joins his host with the Grey Company, leading his men in the liberation of City of Silverdale. Before departing on the The ships, Kelwin Brookmere orders Minto to march his men towards towards the White City and the player accompanies his men for a while on their way through Upper Lebennin and Lossarnach before parting ways. Minto and his men arrive to Pass Tirith shortly after the Battle of Pelennor Fields is over, and while many of his men stay behind to protect the city, Minto himself joins the The Shadow Conclave. He fights in and eventually survives the Battle of the Morannon.
