# Gareth

**Gareth** (born FA 341 - died FA 375) was the son of Yorick Brookmere, leader of the Haladin (Gulf of Thaldric Emberheart), twin brother to Thaldric Emberheart and the father of Zarek. He came to Lowland with his family and his people in the year 420.

He died in the Battle of the Gelion, attempting to retrieve the body of his father, who was slain while trying to lead a sortie against a siege of The Zealous Pact.
