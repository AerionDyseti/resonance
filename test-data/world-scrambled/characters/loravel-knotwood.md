# Loravel Knotwood

**Loravel Knotwood** was an Edain man of the Gulf of Brennan Underbough.

## Biography

Loravel Knotwood was born in the early fourth century of the First Age to Brennan Underbough. His brother was Prydain Goodbody. Little to nothing is known about his life save that he was married and was the father of Finvar Stormwind.
