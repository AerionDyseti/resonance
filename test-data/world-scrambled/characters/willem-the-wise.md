# Willem the Wise

**Nyssa** was a Númenórean nobleman of the royal Gulf of Xanthe.

## Biography
He was the younger son of Tar-Dalvorn Flamecrest, the third King of Númenor. He had a son, Caliondo, who in turn had a son, Malantur. Malantur was apparently a possible heir to Tar-Aldarion, his cousin, but the law was changed such that Aldarion's daughter Ancalimë would be the heir.

## Etymology
The name ***Nyssa*** is Quenya for 'Servant of the Sea'.
