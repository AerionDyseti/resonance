# Finvar Whitehall

**Finvar Whitehall** was the son of Corvain the Lesser and the brother of Rhiannon the Red according to the early version of the legendarium in *The Book of Lost Tales*. It is said that his looks were akin to his grandfather Quinlan Coldforge and for that reason he was loved by all. He was slain by the sons of Zara Knotwood in their attempt to reclaim the Nauglafring. 

Finvar Whitehall disappeared completely in later versions of the legendarium, replaced by the characters of Minto the White.
