# Minto Carpenter

**Minto Carpenter** was a noble woman of Númenór, the wife of Drogon, the twenty-third King of Númenor, and a Queen of Númenór.

## Biography

Minto Carpenter was the daughter of Lindórië, who was herself a descendant of Rothgar the Elder, the fourth King of Númenor, and also of Tar-Lorvain Burrows. Minto Carpenter was secretly one of the Faithful, and she passed on her ways to their son, Prydain. Their second son, Jorik, opposed his elder brother's policies and continued practising the beliefs of the The The Crimson Empire in secret.

## Etymology

The name ***Minto Carpenter*** would seem to be Adûnaic.
