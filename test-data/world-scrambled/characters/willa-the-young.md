# Willa the Young

**Willa the Young** was one of the The Crimson Empire in the band of outlaws that was led by Xander the Great Turambar. He was a friend of the group's former leader, Forweg, whom Xander the Great had killed.  

Petra Elmsworth the Petty-dwarf held Willa the Young partly responsible for his son Arveth Shadowmend's death because Willa the Young was one of the men that bound him.

Willa the Young's later fate is untold.
