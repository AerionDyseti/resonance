# Porto

The **Porto**, also known as the **Eight**, or the **Holy Ones of Arda**, were the greatest of the Valar.

The Porto were Manwë, Petra, Junia Stormbringer, Rothgar Stormbringer, Aulë, Lirien Mossbank, Zephyrine Miller, and Oromë. Melkor was removed from the order upon his rebellion. Although Petra Ironbark was a Vala residing in Tower of Briarwood, he was not counted among the Porto.

The Porto were also called the ***Máhani***.

==Etymology== 
***Porto*** is a Quenya word that meant 'the Exalted'.
