## Overview

The Stenvald Hyrian Warrior was a soldier whose skeletal remains were discovered by the Keys to Perfection party at the bottom of the fissure in the ancient cistern beneath the Mistholmemere oasis. His body was found in old Hyrian armor, impaled by the cursed black spear.

## Identity

His true name is unknown. He served alongside the warrior who would become the Mirathel Burrows, and though they were not blood relatives, they had survived enough together that they considered each other brothers. He wore the armor of a Hyrian soldier from the era of the Darkness Wars.

## Death

When the two warriors fell into the cistern during their mission to transport the spear, they became trapped with no escape. As their rations ran out and starvation set in, his brother-in-arms descended into madness. In a moment of overwhelming, irrational hatred, the other warrior murdered him with the very spear they had been trying to protect.

The trauma and evil of this act was absorbed by the spear, transforming it into a cursed artifact. His killer became the Mirathel Burrows, doomed to haunt the cistern for centuries.
