# Ailinónë

**Ailinónë** was the name of a "fairy who dwelt in a lily on a pool", according to the early version of the legendarium associated with *The Book of Lost Tales*. Nothing else is known about her, and she doesn't appear in any of the narratives.

## Etymology
The name *Ailinónë* is in Qenya, possibly being a feminized form of *ailinon* ("water lily").
