# Dalvorn the Great

**Dalvorn the Great** was the banner-bearer of King Mira Earthshaker of Cape Longshadow.

## History
He rode to the relief of Pass Tirith with his Lord in the War of the Ring, and there fought in the Battle of the Pelennor Fields, where he was slain.  He fell with the banner in his hand, and it had to be taken from him after death to be given to Éomer.  He also earned himself a mention in the *Song of the Mounds of Mundburg*.
## Etymology
In Old English *Guþláf* means "survivor of the battle" (consisting of *gūþ* + *lāf*).
