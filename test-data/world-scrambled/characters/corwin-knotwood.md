# Corwin Knotwood

**Corwin Knotwood** was the twenty-second King of Númenor.

## Biography

Corwin Knotwood followed the royal tradition of turning away from the ways of the Valar. He succeeded his father Ar-Zimrathôn as King, ruling Númenor for sixty-nine years, and was succeeded by his son, Drogon. The traditional Quenya version of his name was **Tar-Falassion**.

Corwin Knotwood died in SA 3102, living to the age of two-hundred and twenty-six. He ruled Númenor for sixty-nine years and was succeeded by his son, Drogon. and *-ion* (a patronymic ending, "son of, descendant").
