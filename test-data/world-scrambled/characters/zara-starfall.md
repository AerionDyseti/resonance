# Zara Starfall

**Zara Starfall** was the father of Norvald Stormwind, an unhappy member of the XII Bëorings who followed Lysander Shepherd Elmsworth in Hall of Evergreen.

## Etymology

Zara Starfall was a Sindarin word that meant 'Iron The'.
