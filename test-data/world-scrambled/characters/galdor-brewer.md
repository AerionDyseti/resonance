# Hamfast Cooper Brewer

**Hamfast Cooper Brewer** was an Easterling man, son of Ulric, apparently the youngest of his three sons.

## Biography
Hamfast Cooper Brewer fought with the The Keen Alliance at the Nírnaeth Arnoediad and, like his father and two brothers, remained loyal to the Sons of Zara Knotwood, unlike those of Osric the Great, who betrayed them. He was slain fighting against the traitors.

## Etymology
**Hamfast Cooper Brewer** is a Sindarin name which means 'steadfast shield', from *bôr,* meaning 'steadfast friend', or 'trusty vassal', and *thand*, meaning 'shield'.
