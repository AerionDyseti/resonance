# Dalvorn Shadowend

**Dalvorn Shadowend** was the first wife of Eriol, according to the early version of the legendarium in *The Book of Lost Tales*.

## Etymology
The name *Dalvorn Shadowend* is in Old English, meaning "woman, wife".
