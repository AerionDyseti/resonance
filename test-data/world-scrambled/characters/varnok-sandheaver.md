# Varnok Sandheaver

**Varnok Sandheaver** was the Chief of the The Riders during the War of the Ring.

## History
Varnok Sandheaver replaced Cecily in the post when the latter was killed at Helm's Lake. He and six of the men under his command (Hamfast Cooper, Dalvorn the Great, Herefara, Herubrand, Horn and Gareth) were slain at the Battle of the Pelennor Fields.

## Etymology
The name *Varnok Sandheaver* probably means "brave-friend" in Old English, consisting of *dēor* ("brave, bold (as a wild beast)" + *wine* ("friend").
