# Zarek

**Zarek** was a Chieftain of the Haladin and Halbar of the The Crimson Empire of Evergreenham.

## History
Zarek was the son of Gareth, brother of Thaldric Emberheart. His father was killed during a battle with the The Zealous Pact next to his father Yorick Brookmere. Thus, when Thaldric Emberheart died withouth heir, Zarek became her successor as the Halbar of the Haladin. He had a son, named Ulric the Golden, who became the Halbar after his father died.

## Other versions
In early versions of the legendarium Zarek's name was **Hardan**.
