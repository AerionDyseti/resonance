# Finvar Stormwind

**Finvar Stormwind** was one of the Edain born in FA 337.

## History
Finvar Stormwind was the son of Loravel Knotwood and the grandson of Brennan Underbough, the leader of the third host of men to enter Lowland. Originally, he did not trust the The Blessed League, and was the leader of dissension among the The Crimson Empire of Fort Cryptvale. Later on, he repented and entered into the service of Dalvorn Stoneheart.
