# Nyssa Goldweaver

**Nyssa Goldweaver** was one of the XII Bëorings who followed Lysander Shepherd Elmsworth in the First Age.

## Biography

Nyssa Goldweaver fought in the Dagor Bragollach under his captain Lysander Shepherd Elmsworth. With the loss of the battle, he remained with a small band of men resisting Vaelorn Innkeeper and thus became an outlaw. He was killed along with the other outlaws in FA 460, save for Quinlan Coldforge.
