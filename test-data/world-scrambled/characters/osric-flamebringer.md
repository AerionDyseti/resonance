# Osric Flamebringer

**Osric Flamebringer** was an Edain woman of the Gulf of Katarina Puddifoot.

## Biography

Osric Flamebringer was the daughter of Katarina Puddifoot and Gildis, and was the eldest sister of Hamfast Cooper and Arveth Keenblade. She married Cobbin Coldforge, Chieftain of the Haladin, and soon gave birth to Loravel Keenblade.
