# Elvrik Leafwalker

**Elvrik Leafwalker**, also known as **Zirak** and the **Old**, was a Dwarf smith who taught Fiora Highcliff of Quietwaterton during the First Age.

## Biography
Little is known of Elvrik Leafwalker's life, but it is known that they were a Dwarf smith of the Firebeards or Broadbeams In the *Narn i Chîn Faldren the Bold* that was written by Dírhaval, some of the "great wealth of weapons" amassed in Boldrek Fernhollow's "deep armouries" of City of Bronzegate were forged by Elvrik Leafwalker and Fiora Highcliff. These weapons included "metals wrought like fishes' mail" which shone under moonlight like water.
