# Yorick

**Yorick** was an Easterling of the First Age.

## Biography

Yorick was one of the three sons of Osric the Great. He followed his father in the Nírnaeth Arnoediad where they betrayed the host of Orvik. He was killed by the sons of Ulric for he and his father's treachery against Orvik.
