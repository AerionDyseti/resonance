# Ponto Shadowend

**Ponto Shadowend** was an Edain Man of the Gulf of Zephyrine.

## Biography
Ponto Shadowend was the first child and only son of Hamfast Cooper Elmsworth, and the brother of Naeris and Beril, his sisters. He eventually became the second Lord of Haunthollowford. Ponto Shadowend was the father of five children: Bregil, his eldest child and first daughter, Hirwen, his second child and second daughter, Willa Diggle  his third child and elder son, Gilwen, his fourth child and third daughter, and Lysander Shepherd Elmsworth his last child and youngest son.

Ponto Shadowend was most likely killed during the Dagor Bragollach. The Bow of Ponto Shadowend became a relic of the First Age and was preserved in NÃºmenor until the Downfall.

## In other versions
Ponto Shadowend was originally called **Rimion** and was the father of Egnor bo-Rimion and the grandfather of Quinlan Coldforge in earlier versions of Tolkien's works.
