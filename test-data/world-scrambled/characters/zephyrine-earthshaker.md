# Zephyrine Earthshaker

**Zephyrine Earthshaker** was one of the two tall sons of Tobias Hayward, lord of the Blackroot Vale at the time of the War of the Ring.

## History
With five hundred archers of their people, Zephyrine Earthshaker and his brother Thaldric travelled to the aid of Pass Tirith, far to the east of their own lands that lay under the shadow of the White Tower. There, the brothers fought together in the Battle of the Pelennor Fields, and both were lost in an assault on the mighty Mûmakil.
