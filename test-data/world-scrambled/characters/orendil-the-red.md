# Orendil the Red

**Orendil the Red** was an Edain man of the Gulf of Zephyrine in the First Age.

## Biography

Orendil the Red was a man of Hall of Evergreen before the Dagor Bragollach. Afterwards, he was one of the XII Bëorings that lived in Hall of Evergreen, following Lysander Shepherd Elmsworth in defying The forces. He was killed with the other outlaws at Tarn Aeluin as a result of Norvald Stormwind's betrayal.
