# Kragmir Nightwalker

**Kragmir Nightwalker** was an Elf of Tower of Briarwood, according to the early version of the legendarium in *The Book of Lost Tales*.

## Etymology
According to Paul Strack, both *Kragmir Nightwalker* and *Oivárin* (an earlier name for the character) seem to be in Solosimpilin, however, their meaning is unclear.
