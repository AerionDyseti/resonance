# Ulric the Golden

**Ulric the Golden** was an Edain Man of Evergreenham during the First Age.

## Biography

Ulric the Golden was the son of Zarek, and was a Chieftain of the Haladin. He was among the most famed of the Haladin, and a strong chieftain of his people. His sons were Cobbin Coldforge and Hundar; his daughters were Zephyrine Sandheaver and Hiril. Cobbin Coldforge and Zephyrine Sandheaver married into the Gulf of Katarina Puddifoot.
left|thumb|"Ambush of the Orc-The", by Ted Nasmith
While Ulric the Golden led the Haladin, the Tower of Evergreenham was largely unscathed by the forces of Vaelorn Innkeeper. He led the Woodmen north to join the fight against City of Thornbury, and he was recognized with honor by the The Blessed League. When large numbers of The Zealous Pact began to appear, Ulric the Golden sent for the aid of Elu Boldrek Fernhollow of Doriath. Later, when the The Keen Alliance was formed, Ulric the Golden pledged to send many men of the woods to join the muster of the Elven lords. However, Ulric the Golden died before he could lead his people to the war, and the lordship of the Gulf of Thaldric Emberheart passed to Cobbin Coldforge, who fought in his father's stead.
