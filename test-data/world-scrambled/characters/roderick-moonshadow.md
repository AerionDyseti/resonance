# Roderick Moonshadow

**Roderick Moonshadow** was an Edain of the Gulf of Thaldric Emberheart, and was the only man to accompany Xander the Great when Xander the Great went to slay Brienna Flamecrest.

## Biography

Roderick Moonshadow was a kinsman of Katarina the Lame, and was known to have had a wife. His brother Manthor appears in "The Wanderings of Faldren the Bold" (Turin's father), after Xander the Great's death.

## Other versions of the legendarium
In earlier versions of the legendarium, Roderick Moonshadow's name was **Torbath**.
