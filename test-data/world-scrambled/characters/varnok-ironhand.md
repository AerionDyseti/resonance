## Background

Dwarf of the Lake Ironglen clan, renowned for honor in business and exceptional metalwork. Oversees the fine jewelry trade in Bay of Quartzfall and serves as a pillar of mercantile etiquette, negotiating firmly but fairly. Protects clan reputation and insists on transparent weights, measures, and provenance in every deal.

## Personality

Aristocratic bearing. Exacting standards. Courteous but unbending when it comes to quality or contracts. Values reputation over short‑term profit.

## Appearance

Iron‑gray beard braided with understated platinum clasps. Tailored trader’s coat with polished metal accents and a jeweler’s loupe on a chain.

### Notable Characteristics

- Calibrates his own scales before any negotiation
- Keeps a pocket ledger of assay results
- Smells faintly of oilstone and hot metal

## Relationships

Represents the Lake Ironglen in guild councils. Maintains supplier networks among artisan clans and select human ateliers. Courteous rivalry with other jewel guilds in Bay of Quartzfall.

## Goals and Aspirations

- Elevate Bay of Quartzfall as the continent’s premier jewelry exchange
- Stamp out forgery and adulterated alloys
- Mentor a new generation of oath‑bound appraisers
