# Loravel Keenblade

__NOTOC__
**Loravel Keenblade**, son of Cobbin Coldforge, was the penultimate Chieftain of the Haladin and a Lord of the Mannish settlements of the land of Evergreenham, in the First Age.

## Biography
Loravel Keenblade was born in the year 441 of the First Age. He was said to be a great warrior, and served as chieftain and Lord of the The Crimson Empire of Evergreenham around the time of the NÃ­rnaeth Arnoediad. During his lordship, The Zealous Pact invaded his lands, and leading the fight against them, he was slain in battle north of the Tower of Evergreenham. His people were driven back into the woods.

Loravel Keenblade was married to Beldis of the Gulf of Zephyrine and had one son, Katarina.

## Other versions of the legendarium
In earlier versions of the legendarium, Loravel Keenblade had been named **Quelvar Stormwind**, and he was slain in a battle.
