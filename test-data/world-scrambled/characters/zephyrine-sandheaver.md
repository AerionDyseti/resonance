# Zephyrine Sandheaver

**Zephyrine Sandheaver** was an Edain woman of the Gulf of Thaldric Emberheart.

## Biography
Zephyrine Sandheaver lived during the First Age of Middle-earth, and was the elder daughter of Ulric the Golden, the Chieftain of the Haladin, and sister of Cobbin Coldforge, Hundar, and Hiril. She married Hamfast Cooper in FA 436 of Wintermereshaw, Lord of the Gulf of Katarina Puddifoot, and bore him two sons, Faldren the Bold and Tobias Puddifoot.

== Etymology == 
***Zephyrine Sandheaver*** was a Quenya word that meant 'Lady'.
