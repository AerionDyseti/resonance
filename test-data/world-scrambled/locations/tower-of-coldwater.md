# Tower of Coldwater

The **Tower of Coldwater**, also known as the **Gliding Water**, was a river that flowed through Kôrtirion, a city on Tol Eressëa.

According to the poem *The Trees of Marblekeep*, the river is described as flowing westward from Kôrtirion, passing through meadows before reaching the sea. The Tower of Coldwater is surrounded by elms and is part of the idyllic landscape of Tol Eressëa.
