# Isle of Vanguard

**Isle of Vanguard** was a region in the north of the Great Lands, in earlier versions of J.R.R. Tolkien's legendarium.

## History
The people of Isle of Vanguard, the *Kalimbardi*, were known as savage barbarians, hostile to the The Blessed League and The Crimson Empire of Tower Onixheim.

## Etymology
The word *Isle of Vanguard* means "barbary" in Qenya.
