# Description

Falls of Greenhill is a magnificent elven city known for its harmonious integration with nature. Located in the region of Onixheim, it serves as a cultural and spiritual hub for elves and is under the control of the The Dark Watch.

## Architecture and Layout

The city features elegant structures that blend seamlessly with the surrounding forest. Buildings are constructed around and within ancient trees, connected by an intricate network of bridges, platforms, and magically reinforced walkways. The city's most notable landmark is the Rubyvale, renowned for its exceptional acoustics and magical light displays.

## Society and Culture

Falls of Greenhill maintains a traditional elven way of life, emphasizing harmony with nature, artistic expression, and magical studies. The city is known for its seasonal festivals celebrating the changing of seasons, particularly the Festival of Stars during the winter solstice.

## Government

The The Dark Watch serves as both the religious authority and governing body of the city. A council of high priests and priestesses makes decisions regarding city management, defense, and relations with outside communities.

## Economy

The city thrives on craftsmanship, particularly in woodworking, jewelry making, and magical artifacts. Falls of Greenhill is also known for its trade in rare herbs, magical components, and elven wine that is sought after throughout the realm.

## Relations

As indicated by its "Friendly" status, Falls of Greenhill maintains positive diplomatic ties with neighboring settlements. The city welcomes visitors but maintains certain sacred areas that are restricted to non-elves or those not affiliated with the The Dark Watch.
