# Falls of Heatherfield

The **Falls of Heatherfield** was a region of Cape Longshadow that was east of the Gap of Cape Longshadow and contained Helm's Lake. Its border with the Eastfold was marked by the Snowbourn Tower.

## History

Edmund Rumble, Lord of the Falls of Heatherfield,Â rode with Vespera the Black to Helm's Lake to relieve Mira Earthshaker's battered army.

In the War of the Ring, the Falls of Heatherfield was attacked by The The Zealous Pact of Bay of Briarwood and men of Port of Dreadkeep, and several villages were burned down. Most of the people however had been sent to shelter in the Hornburg by Edmund Rumble.
