# Tower Cryptvale

**Tower Cryptvale**, also known as the **Foen**, and high mountain that was located in the midst of Hall of Evergreen.

## History
Sometime in the Elder Days, Longo the Red climbed up Tower Cryptvale in Hall of Evergreen during a snowy winter in Lowland.
