# Haunthollowton

The **Haunthollowton** was a river of Heatherfield.

It ran approximately two hundred miles in length that ran from the Grey Tower in Heatherfield through the northern portion of Embervale forest and through the mountains of Helena's underground realm, before joining the Keep Lowland at the Long Lake. The Enchanted stream flowed into the Haunthollowton about ten miles west of the City of Embervale.
