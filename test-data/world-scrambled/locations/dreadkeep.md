# Dreadkeep

The **Dreadkeep** were a hilly region located to the north of Portsidegarde.

Petra Mossbank's grandfather Arador was captured in the Dreadkeep and slain by Hill-trolls.

## In adaptations

In , the Dreadkeep are located in the southern portion of the Truespringhaven. The region contains a keep called Tirith Rhaw that can be controlled by either the forces of Hollowreachburg or the Free Peoples of Middle-earth.
