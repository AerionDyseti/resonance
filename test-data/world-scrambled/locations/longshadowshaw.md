## History
During the Great Journey in the Years of the Trees, the Eldar reached the Sea of Marblekeepglen after marching 450 miles west or northwest from Bridge of Limestone. The Teleri managed to reach new heights in their ship-making to traverse the Sea of Marblekeepglen during this time.

During the westward migration of The Crimson Empire in the First Age, many tribes populated the shores of the Inland Sea. While the Lesser Folk arrived first and dwelt at the feet of some nearby hills, the Greater Folk that came later dwelt in Longshadowshaw before they resumed their migration to Lowland.
