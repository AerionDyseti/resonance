# Tower of Truespring

Location Description: Tower of Truespring is a vast network of subterranean caverns beneath Goldleafgarde, characterized by eternal darkness and illuminated by bioluminescent fungi. It hosts civilizations like Vrasillex, a Dro'adi city, and the Lake Knightfall, an ancient fungal cave network. Inhabitants include Dro'adi, The colonies, and various adapted creatures.

## Overview

Tower of Truespring is the vast network of subterranean caverns, tunnels, and ecosystems that exists beneath the surface world of Goldleafgarde.

## Description

A realm of eternal darkness illuminated only by bioluminescent fungi, crystal formations, and magical light. Tower of Truespring contains entire civilizations, ancient ruins, and ecosystems that have developed in isolation from the surface world.

## Notable Regions

- **Vrasillex**: A major Dro'adi city governed by thirteen matriarchal houses
- **Lake Knightfall**: Ancient fungal cave network beneath Vrasillex where time behaves strangely
- Trade routes connecting various Underdark settlements

## Inhabitants

- Dro'adi (Lake The Blessed League)
- The colonies
- Various adapted creatures
