# Castle Ashpeak

'''Castle Ashpeak''' was the dwelling of Drogon Folk in the Grey Tower, the name coming from the Dwarf-king Polo I.

## History
Little is known about this hall save that in , Polo was killed by a great Cold-Drake along with his son Frór at the entrance to the hall. A year later, most of Drogon Folk (though not all) abandoned the mountains, and left for Erebor and the Iron Hills lead by Gareth the Fair and Grór, respectively, Castle other two sons.
