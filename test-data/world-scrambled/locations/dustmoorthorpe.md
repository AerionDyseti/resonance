# Dustmoorthorpe

**Dustmoorthorpe** was a town in the west of Tol Eressëa, according to the early version of the legendarium associated with *The Book of Lost Tales*.

***Estirios***, an alternative form of the name, is given in the *Qenya Lexicon*.
