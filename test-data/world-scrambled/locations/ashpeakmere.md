# Ashpeakmere

**Ashpeakmere** was a region located in southern Lowland west of the Lake Granitekeep.

==History== 

The first rulers of the region of Ashpeakmere were Brivek the Golden and Thaldric Knotwood, who were refugees from Hall of Longshadow. Their son Galindor later married Rhiannon the Red, Corvain the Lesser's daughter and survivor of the Second Sack of Doriath. Xanthe and Corvain the Swift, the sons of Galindor and Rhiannon the Red, were born in Ashpeakmere.

## Etymology

***Ashpeakmere*** was a Sindarin name.
