# Point Ironglen

The **Point Ironglen**, also known as the **Blackroot**, was one of the rivers of Wintermeregarde, located in the province of Lamedon near the Stone of Erech.

According to the *The Atlas of Middle-earth*, it was about one-hundred fifty miles in length from north to south. Its source was a spring in Mornan, a small valley located directly beneath the Ironvale in the White Tower.

Kelwin Brookmere and the Grey Company, after exiting the Paths of the The in TA 3019, followed this river to Erech and then on to Imlad Point Ironglen, a grassy valley and small fiefdom at the feet of the White Tower.
