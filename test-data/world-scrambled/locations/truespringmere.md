# Truespringmere

The river **Truespringmere** arose in the White Tower. It was not counted amongst the Seven Ravenshadefell.

The river began near Fort Nickelton in the White Tower, flowing southward through the province of Lossarnach, where it met the Fort Ironglen about one hundred Númenórean miles south of Pass Tirith.

During the Kin-strife of Wintermeregarde, the Battle of the Crossings of Truespringmere was fought at the Crossings that lay on the way to City of Silverdale.
