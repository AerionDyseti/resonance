# Truespringhaven

The **Truespringhaven**, also known as the **Troll-fells**, were a highland region of Port of Limestone, which was situated west of the Misty Tower. It was located north of Portsidegarde and the Dreadkeep, and was possibly near Lowlandgarde. The land was wild, and many evil creatures dwelt there. It was also said to be the original home of the trolls Zephyrine the Bold.

## History
The Truespringhaven had been a peaceful part of the DÃºnedain kingdom of Arnor until the middle of the Third Age when, during the war with Hollowreachburg, the breakaway kingdom of Rhudaur became a vassal of the Valdric Nightshade. After the war, the Valdric fled to the Truespringhaven after his defeat at the Battle of Valley. At that time the Truespringhaven were mainly ruled by Hill-men and The Zealous Pact, and was infested with trolls.
