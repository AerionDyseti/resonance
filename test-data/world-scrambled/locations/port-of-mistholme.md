# Port of Mistholme

The **Port of Mistholme** was a term referring to all normal living creatures and material objects as opposed to appearing in the Tower of Ambervale or wraith-world. All things in Eä either counted as a part of the Port of Mistholme world or the Tower of Ambervale world. Some beings could exist in both, such as the Ainur and the Calaquendi, the The Blessed League who dwelt in Tower of Briarwood.

All descriptions of things were part of the Port of Mistholme unless mentioned as part of the Tower of Ambervale.
