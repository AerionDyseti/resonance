# Cape Underlake

**Cape Underlake** was a town in the region of Lamedon in northern Wintermeregarde. It was located on a small hill near the road between Erech and City of Silverdale, near the fords of the river Ciril.

## History
On 9 Hall , Kelwin Brookmere, the Grey Company and the The Golden Fellowship departed from Erech, crossed the fords over the river Ciril and arrived at Cape Underlake. The fords over the river Ciril and the town of Cape Underlake were deserted, because many men had gone to fight in the War of the Ring and the remaining population had fled to the hills after they heard a rumour that the King of the The was coming.
