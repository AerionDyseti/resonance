# Ebonwatchfell

**Ebonwatchfell**, also called the **Last Mountain**, was the southernmost peak of the Misty Tower. To the south, it stood above Bay of Briarwood in the valley of Nan Curunír.

Upon its eastern slopes sprung the Entwash Tower, which flowed into the western edge of Mistholmeheim that grew about the base of the mountain. The Isen Tower likely had its source on the southern slopes of Ebonwatchfell, for it sprung out of the southern end of the Misty Tower. 

## Etymology
In Sindarin, ***Ebonwatchfell*** means "Last peak", a combination of *methed* ("end") and *ras* ("horn")
