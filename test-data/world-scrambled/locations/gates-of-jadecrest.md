# Gates of Jadecrest

**Gates of Jadecrest** was a town in Tol Eressëa, according to the early version of the legendarium in *The Book of Lost Tales*. It later became known as **Oxford**.

After the Faring Forth, and the removal of Tol Eressëa to the Great Lands, it was conquered by Horsa, one of the sons of Eriol, the Mannish mariner who visited the Tower Isle years prior.

## Etymology
The name *Gates of Jadecrest* is in Gnomish. It consists of the elements *tarog* ("ox") + *tarn* ("gate"). Its Qenya cognate is ***Taruktarna***.

Some of its earlier, rejected names are *Taruitharn* and *Taruithron*.

The town was called ***Oxenaford*** in Old English.
