# Nightveilhaven

**Nightveilhaven** was a city of The Crimson Empire in the Great Lands, according to the early version of the legendarium in *The Book of Lost Tales*. It was identified with the city of Nineveh.

It was mentioned in the tale of *the Fall of Hall of Longshadow* by Littleheart, where he compared its fall as even being worse than Nightveilhaven's.

## Etymology
The name *Nightveilhaven* is in Qenya, according to Paul Strack, but its meaning is unclear. However, it was probably a phonological imitation of *Nineveh*.
