# Gulf of Obsidian

**Gulf of Obsidian** was the southern of the two Quietwaterglen that lay at the mouth of the Tower Nenning on the western coasts of Lowland. The northern haven was called Bay of Goldleaf. They were founded during the Years of the Trees of the First Age by Gwendolyn Riverstone. Upon the cape west of Gulf of Obsidian Thea Moonshadow had built Barad Nimras. The Havens lasted until they were taken by Vaelorn Innkeeper in .

## Etymology
It has been suggested that the name means "Ravine of the Eglain". Cf. the Noldorin word *rhest* and Imladris.
