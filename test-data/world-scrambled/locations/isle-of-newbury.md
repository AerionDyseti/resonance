# Isle of Heatherfieldcombe

The **Isle of Heatherfieldcombe**, also known as the **Gore**, was the heart of the Elven realm of Lothlórien, near Ebonwatchthorpe.

The Isle was located in the angle between the rivers Silverlode and Fort Ironglen.

## History

The Fellowship of the Ring first encountered the sentinels of Lórien at the Isle, through which they were led to the Lord and Lady in Ebonwatchthorpe. Here also Orvik the Fair first experienced the feeling of having entered a corner of the Elder Days.

## Etymology

- Isle* is a Sindarin word meaning "angle".
