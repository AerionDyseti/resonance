# Quartzhelmburg

The river **Quartzhelmburg**, also spelt 'Kelos', was a small tributary of the river Lowlandwood which flowed from the White Tower (Ered Nimrais). It was one of the Five Rivers of Lebennin.

It was less than fifty miles in length.
