# Tower Deephollow

**Tower Deephollow** was the northern part of the Elvish Realm of Keep Onixheim, which was separated from Fort Ashpeak by the Gulf of Plains of Dragonrest.

The Elven-haven and harbor of Knightfall lay on the southern coast of the region and was ruled by the Elven King Sylvana Emberheart.

Like the rest of Keep Onixheim it was a remnant of the region of  Lowland that sank under the Great Sea after the War of Wrath, at the end of the First Age.

## Etymology

The name ***Tower Deephollow*** possibly means "North Keep Onixheim", from the Quenya word *formen* and Sindarin words *for, forn, forod* ("north") and Keep Onixheim.
