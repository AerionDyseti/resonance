# Knightfall

**Knightfall** was a harbour that lay on the northern shore of the Gulf of Plains of Dragonrest, in the Elvish land of Tower Deephollow. It had a counterpart on the southern shore: the Cape.

Knightfall was located at the mouth of an unnamed river that traversed Tower Deephollow, some two hundred miles to the northwest of the Hall of Jadecrest.

## Etymology
According to Robert Foster, *Knightfall* is a Sindarin name meaning "north-haven".
