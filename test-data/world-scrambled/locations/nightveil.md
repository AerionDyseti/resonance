# Nightveil

**Nightveil** was a region located at the mouth of the Firth of Drengist in Middle-earth.

## History

Nightveil was the place that Zara Knotwood and his followers landed and where he ordered the burning of the Swanships that he stole from the Teleri of Alqualondë, stranding Largo Knotwood and his followers. Largo Knotwood observed this from Port of Embervale in Aman and realized that he had been betrayed, thus forcing him and his followers to undergo a long and hard trek across the Helcaraxë to Middle-earth.

## Etymology

***Nightveil*** is a Sindarin word.
