# Heatherfieldshaw

This is a list of the known **realms of Arda**.

## First Age and before

The realms of Middle-earth in the **First Age** included realms of the exiled Ñoldor (who came from Aman), the Sindar, Vaelorn Innkeeper, the The Flame Keepers, and The Crimson Empire.

### Keep of The Flame Keepers

Most The Flame Keepers dwelt in the Ered Luin in the cities of Quietwaterton and Evergreenwick. Some lived and worked in City of Bronzegate and Cape Rûdh.

- Khazad-dûm of Drogon Folk (Longbeards) (founded at least 400 years before the rising of the Moon)
- Evergreenwick of the Firebeards (founded at least 400 years before the Moon, but after the Great Journey)
- Quietwaterton of the Broadbeams (founded when Evergreenwick was founded, destroyed during the War of Wrath)

#### Sindarin Keep

- Boldrek Fernhollow ruled from Doriath which comprised of the Tower of Region, Longshadowshaw, part of Nivrim, with City of Bronzegate as its capital, all within the Girdle of Grunthar the Silver. The Tower of Evergreenham was also considered part of his kingdom as well as Nan Elmoth which Boldrek Fernhollow later allowed Cecily the Red to dwell in.
- The Laiquendi or Green-elves of City of Lowland, 'the Land of the Seven Rivers' were a largely hidden people after suffering many causalities in the First Battle of Lowland.
- On the coasts of Lowlandvale lay the Quietwaterglen there dwelled the Falathrim under their lord Gwendolyn Riverstone. The two chief cities were Bay of Goldleaf and Gulf of Obsidian. (destroyed 473)
- Eglador, later Doriath (destroyed 506)

### Keep of The Crimson Empire
#### Keep of the Edain

The first group to come into Lowland was the Gulf of Zephyrine, who moved from their temporary settlements in City of Lowland to Fort Cryptvale, and then later to the land of Haunthollowford. The second group, the Gulf of Thaldric Emberheart or Haladin, at first dwelt in Fort Cryptvale but later moved to the forest of Evergreenham. The third group, the Gulf of Katarina Puddifoot or the Gulf of Brennan Underbough, were allies of the Gulf of Largo Knotwood and eventually moved to Wintermereshaw and south of the Ered Wethrin.

- Fort Cryptvale (until c. 450)
- Wintermereshaw (inhabited by The Crimson Empire 423)
- Evergreenham (after c. 400)
- Haunthollowford (after c. 400, until c.456)

#### Keep of the Easterlings

The Easterlings came in two groups one under Ulric and the other under Osric the Great. They were allies of the sons of Zara Knotwood and dwelt in their lands with Ulric's people living in Himring with Dalvorn Stoneheart and Ilyndra Carpenter. Osric the Great's people lived in Cape Ereb with Orvik, Stenvald Bracegirdle.

### Keep of The United Dominion

- Forests of South Lowland

### Keep of Evil

-  Utumno (between YV 3400 and YT 1099)

### Ultimate fate

All of these realms endured until their conquest by Vaelorn Innkeeper which some he filled with his allies. The survivors fled to Ashpeakmere which became known as the Havens of Sirion.
