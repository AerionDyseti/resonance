# Onixheim

Contains: Dustmoor (Falls%20Balenir%201f9ca0aafe2181f4821fe593358f3b13.md), Ironglenwood (Dragon's%20Den%201fcca0aafe2180e28c5cd0e83c06fd36.md), Castle Marblekeep (Castle%20Cabin%201fcca0aafe2180d6b17cc9e0e1222f33.md), Falls of Greenhill (Falls%20Anastrael%20216ca0aafe218027b1c8da3e0a0161b3.md)
Part Of: Briarwood (Briarwood%201f9ca0aafe218151abacec629dcc4892.md)
Location Description: Ancient Elven forest kingdom home to both the Amtreadi (High The Blessed League) and Liinadi (Wood The Blessed League), serving as the political and magical center of Elven civilization. The kingdom has existed since approximately 6000 MR and survived the Great Sundering (~5200 MR) when High The Blessed League nearly exterminated the Lake The Blessed League, splitting the Elven peoples. Currently a theocracy led by Zaldric Stormbringer, Speaker of the The Blessed League, operating under Anastrasael's (The Starfather's) guidance. Notable features include ancient elven forests, magical academies, the Hall of Secrets (political and knowledge repository), celestial observatories for divine guidance, and sacred sites marking important historical locations.

Elven Tower and home of the Liinadi (wood elves) and the Amtreadi (high elves).
