# Pass of Onixheim

alt=|thumb|300px|The Pass of Onixheim in Peter Jackson's __NOTOC__
The hills of **Pass of Onixheim**, upon either side of Nen Hithoel, were a vast, seemingly impenetrable maze of crags situated in the region of Heatherfield in Middle-earth.

The Pass of Onixheim lay between the Eastemnet of Cape Longshadow and the relatively empty lands that contained the Quartzhelmhaven and the Nindalf. It was approximately seventy-five miles from east to west and some one-hundred miles from north to south. The western boundary of Pass of Onixheim and eastern border of Cape Longshadow were formed by the Castle Wall of Cape Longshadow, a great, natural rocky cliff running in a curve from north to south.

## History
The Pass of Onixheim had once marked the northern borders of the The of Wintermeregarde at its height.

In TA 3002, Éomund, father of Éomer and Éowyn, was slain while pursuing a band of The Zealous Pact near Pass of Onixheim.

At the beginning of , Orvik the Fair and Cecily Ashford, attempting to reach the Black Gate of Plains of Pebblebrook, are lost in the eastern Pass of Onixheim for days until Ponto the Grey finds them. After a great deal of persuasion, he agrees to show them the way to the Gate, leading them south into the Quartzhelmhaven.
