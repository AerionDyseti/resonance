# Sindanórië

***Sindanórië***, or ***Sindanóriello***, was the "name of a mythical region of shadows lying at outer feet of the Tower of Tower of Briarwood" in Aman.

## History
Sindanórië was briefly mentioned in "Namárië", the lament that Barlo Lightfoot composed in the Third Age. In the poem, darkness flowed out of Sindanórië to lie on the waves separating Barlo Lightfoot and Petra.

## Etymology
- Sindanórië* is a name in Quenya meaning "land of greyness",
