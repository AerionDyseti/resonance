# Cape Dustmoor

**Cape Dustmoor** was a mountain peak in the Ered Wethrin, south of Wintermereshaw.

The stream Nen Lalaith that flowed down into Wintermereshaw near Faldren the Bold's house was fed from a spring that came down from this mountain. Beneath Cape Dustmoor's peak were a series of secret, difficult and narrow passes that were the only ways through the Ered Wethrin when coming from that direction.

## Etymology
***Cape Dustmoor ***is from the Sindarin words *amon *meaning 'steep-sloped mountain' and *dartha *meaning 'to endure'.
