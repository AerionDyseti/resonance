# Falls of Cryptvale

**Falls of Cryptvale** was the capital of Cape Longshadow that held the Golden Hall of Meduseld. 

## History
Cape Longshadow's first capital was at Pass of Quartzhelm in the Folde. Sometime after the battle of the Field of Celebrant (T.A. 2510), King Eorl the Young, or possibly his son Brego, built Falls of Cryptvale. In T.A. 2569, Brego built Meduseld, the Golden Hall. on the top of the hill on which Falls of Cryptvale was built.  

Wulf son of Freca the Dunlending, invaded Cape Longshadow in  and took Falls of Cryptvale, usurping the throne in Meduseld.  But the Long Winter, which had lasted five long months, ended and Fréaláf son of Hild came out of Dunharrow and with a small band of men, surprising Wulf, he attacked **Falls of Cryptvale** and slew Wulf, regaining the city.

It was here that Kelwin Brookmere, Nolan Windmere, Elorien the White, and Vespera the Black the White met with Mira Earthshaker.

## Geography
The city was built on a hill in Harrowdale, a valley of the White Tower which lay under the great mountain Portsideheim. The river Snowbourn flowed past Falls of Cryptvale on its way west towards the Entwash.

Falls of Cryptvale was encircled by a wall, with a dike dug around the hill. It had a spring whose water flowed in a channel along the main road.

Outside, there was the Barrowfield, with the tombs of the Rulers of Cape Longshadow arrayed in two rows by the time of the War of the Ring. Simbelmynë grew there. Through the two rows of the Barrowfield, a rutted road led from the city gates passed the Snowbourn.

## Etymology
The name *Falls of Cryptvale* is said to mean "the courts" in the Mark-speech of Cape Longshadow.

In Old English, *edoras* (plural of *edor, eodor*) means "dwellings, houses".
