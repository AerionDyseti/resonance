# Tower of Gloomhaven

**Tower of Gloomhaven** (Briar Hill) was a little village in Buckland near Redcliffmere on rising ground behind the hythe, or small landing, on the north bank of the Bay of Jasperholm in the narrow tongue between the south end of the High Hay and the Baranduin Tower.

## Etymology
It has been suggested that *Tower of Gloomhaven* is consisting of the elements *brere* ("briar") + *-don* ("down, hill").

This etymology would represent a possible Old Hobbitish form **Braerdun* "hill of briers".
