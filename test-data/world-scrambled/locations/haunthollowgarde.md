# Haunthollowgarde

The **Haunthollowgarde** was a region north of Portsidegarde, contained within the Truespringhaven and adjacent to the Dreadkeep.

Petra Mossbank Elessar knew the region to by inhabited by trolls during his time as a ranger, though he was otherwise unfamiliar with it.

## Etymology

***Haunthollowgarde*** literally means 'giant-valleys'. It is unclear if the location was in fact a home to giants.

- Etten* comes from the Old English *eoten*, meaning *City*.
