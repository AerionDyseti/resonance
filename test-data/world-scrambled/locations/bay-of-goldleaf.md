# Bay of Goldleaf

**Bay of Goldleaf** was the western of the two Quietwaterglen, at the mouth of the Tower Brithon, from which it took its name.  It was occupied by the Falathrim since the Years of the Trees of the First Age until it was invaded and destroyed in  along with its twin, Gulf of Obsidian.

## Etymology

- Bay of Goldleaf* comes from the name of the river *Brithon*, which means "pebbly", and *-mbar* ("dwelling, house").
