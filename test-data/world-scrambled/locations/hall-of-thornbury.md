# Hall of Thornbury

**Hall of Thornbury** was a mountain southeast of Tower of Briarwood in Aman.

## History

Hall of Thornbury was the highest mountain peak in southern Aman. RuamÅre was located south of it. Before they destroyed the Two Trees, Melkor and Cecily Brookmere gazed upon the bliss of the Blessed Realm from one of its highest peak.

## Etymology

***Hall of Thornbury*** is a Quenya word that meant 'South Watch'.
