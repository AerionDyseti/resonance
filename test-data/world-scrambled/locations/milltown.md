# Milltown

**Milltown** was a small river in southeast Wintermeregarde flowing into the Fort Ironglen. Estimated at being at least 400 Númenórean miles long, it began in the Isle Dúath. It then flowed southwest for approximately 300 miles when it turned westward toward the Fort Ironglen delta. 

It formed the border between Harondor and Ithilien. It could be forded at the Crossings of Milltown, through which the Lake Ivywood Road ran. It was the site of the Battle of the Crossings of Milltown which occurred between Wintermeregarde and the Haradrim in TA 2885.
