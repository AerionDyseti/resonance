# Eternum

**i·Ponōrir** was a region in the far north of the Great Lands in earlier versions of J.R.R. Tolkien's legendarium.

## History
i·Ponōrir was the land where the Gates of Dustmoor came from.

## Etymology
- i·Ponōrir* is a Qenya name glossed as "the Northlands, Scandinavia".

- Pŏnōre* is the Qenya name of a region in i·Ponōrir that is glossed as Norway.
