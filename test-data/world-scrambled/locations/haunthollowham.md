# Haunthollowham

The **Haunthollowham**, also known as the **Black Fence**, was a mountainous ridge in the Isle Dúath (Tower of Shadow) which formed a defensive inner wall in Plains of Pebblebrook.

Between it and the Isle Dúath lay a road which ran between Durthang and the Morgul Pass. Orvik the Fair and Cecily Ashford followed this road north for a while after the most direct crossing of the Haunthollowham was blocked by a Nazgûl.
