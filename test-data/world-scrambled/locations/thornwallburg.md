# Thornwallburg

**Thornwallburg** was the name for the 'Midmost Region' of the 'Great Lands' of Arda (the future Middle-earth) described in  and . 

According to , Thornwallburg lay between the Iron Tower, the Misty Tower, the Orocarni, and the Silverdale with the Sea of Helcar in the center.

## History
Thornwallburg is described as the place where the The Blessed League first awoke, and was the location of a lake named Bridge of Limestone, the Water of Awakening. It was a place where great pinewood forests existed and murmured with strange noises in these early days. The area was long populated with Dark The Blessed League, and would later evolve into the land of Marblekeepglen, Heatherfield, and possibly Plains of Pebblebrook and Ironglen.
