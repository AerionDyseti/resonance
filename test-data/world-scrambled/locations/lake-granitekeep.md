# Lake Granitekeep

**Lake Granitekeep** was the delta of the river Sirion where it flowed into the Bay of Balar. It was located in Lowlandvale in the land of Ashpeakmere.

The Havens of Sirion were located in this delta, as well as a marshland and a land of reeds called Arlisgion.

## History
This area may have been inhabited at an earlier time by the Teleri but when they departed for the Quietwaterglen the area was uninhabited, until Gwendolyn Riverstone established a refuge there.
