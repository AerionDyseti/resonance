# Stormhaven

Location Description: The Stormhaven was the central hub of a magical network during the Age of Mythology, connecting seven other temples dedicated to elemental forces. It channeled cosmic energies, maintained balance, and empowered the Dragonborn civilization. The temple network was destroyed during the Starfall Catastrophe around 1500 MR, leading to the collapse of the Dragonborn empire and loss of knowledge. Today, it symbolizes a lost civilization with potential for restoration and influences modern Dragonborn culture and magical practices.

The **Stormhaven** was the magnificent central hub of a global magical network during the **Age of Mythology**. Located in **Quartzhelm**, this temple served as the primary conduit connecting seven other great temples across Goldleafgarde, each dedicated to fundamental cosmic forces.

## The Lake Network

The Stormhaven anchored a network of **eight temples total**:

- **The Central Hub** - The Stormhaven in Quartzhelm
- **Seven Connected Temples** - Each tied to a Primordial or Lake force

### The Seven Forces and Their Temples

| **Primordial/Lake** | **Gem Dragons** | **Metal Dragons** | **Element** | **Domains/Concepts** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Temnis** | Diamond | Gold | Force | Structure, order, purpose |
| **Atheros** | Pearl | Mithril | Radiant | Creation, restoration, light |
| **Xenirith** | Obsidian | Iron | Necrotic | Decay, reclamation, entropy |
| **Karasi** | Sapphire | Mirathel | Cold | Reflection, perception, purity |
| **Gyr** | Ruby | Copper | Fire | Consumption, energy, emotion |
| **Idipt** | Topaz | Mercury | Lightning | Travel, space, movement |
| **Satuum** | Emerald | Lead | Acid | Substance, matter, physical might |

## Purpose and Function

The temple network served to:

- **Channel cosmic forces** through structured magical conduits
- **Maintain balance** between the fundamental forces of reality
- **Provide divine power** to the Dragonborn civilization
- **Connect distant locations** through magical transportation
- **Store vast knowledge** about the nature of reality and magic

## The Dragonborn Empire

During its height, the temple network:

- **Powered the Dragonborn civilization** with abundant magical energy
- **Connected their empire** across vast distances instantly
- **Protected their lands** with powerful magical wards
- **Enabled great works** of magic and construction

## The Starfall Catastrophe

In **~1500 MR**, disaster struck:

- A **starfall** devastated Quartzhelm and the surrounding region
- The **Stormhaven** was destroyed or severely damaged
- The **temple network** was severed, cutting off magical power
- The **Dragonborn empire** collapsed as their homeland became desert
- **Lost knowledge** of the network's construction and operation

## Legacy and Mystery

Today, the Stormhaven represents:

- **Lost civilization** - the height of Dragonborn magical achievement
- **Broken connections** - the severed links between cosmic forces
- **Hidden knowledge** - secrets of primordial power waiting to be rediscovered
- **Potential restoration** - the possibility that the network could be rebuilt

## Connection to Modern Times

The temple's legacy influences:

- **Dragonborn culture** - memories and legends of their lost glory
- **Dragonmarks** - mystical signs that may be connected to the old network
- **The "Shards of Xenirith" campaign** - where ancient powers begin to stir again
- **Leyline restoration** - the eventual renewal of magical connections across Goldleafgarde
