# Dragonrestglen

Part Of: Fort Wintermere (Fort Wintermere%201f9ca0aafe2181f18b58de0125bf07e2.md)
Location Description: City forest nation home to the Raptoran bird-folk, featuring some of the tallest trees in the world and creating a stratified society between flying and flightless Raptorans. Created by Corus (The Wind-Caller) as homeland for his Raptoran creations. The massive trees create natural stratification between flying Aaracokra living in the canopies and flightless Kenku dwelling on the forest floor. Notable features include aerial cities in canopies, ground settlements, stratified Raptoran society, and a giant forest ecosystem.
